Carmen Jorda dons a plunging white gown at the 2023 Laureus World Sport Awards in Paris

Publish date: 2024-04-02

Carmen Jorda wowed onlookers as she walked the red carpet at the 2023 Laureus World Sport Awards on Tuesday in Paris.

The Spanish racing driver, 34, flashed her cleavage in a plunging white gown featuring a very lengthy train comprised of large bows.

She accessorised her look with some dazzling silver jewellery and had flawlessly applied a full face of make-up to highlight her natural beauty.

Carmen wore her glossy blonde locks in a sleek side-parting and posed up a storm for photographers. 

Upon her arrival at the Cour Vendome, the sportswoman ensured all eyes were on her while smouldering for her camera lenses.
