Emma Stone in fake orgasm so rampant she thought she was dying during filming

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Emma Stone once faked an orgasm so wild that she thought she was “dying” during it.

The Poor Things star shot to fame after starring in 2010 coming-of-age comedy Easy A, loosely based on Nathaniel Hawthorne’s 1850 novel The Scarlet Letter. Emma’s character Olive had to fake an orgasm so loud during fake sex with her closeted friend Brandon that it shook the walls.

The plan was to make it look as though the pair were sleeping together to get the bullies off Brandon’s back, so the pair decided to simulate sex while at a house party. But behind the scenes, their raunchy antics led Emma to a real life-changing diagnosis.
