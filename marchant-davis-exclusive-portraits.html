Marchnt Davis Exclusive Portraits Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-02-23

Marchánt Davis is an American actor who most recently starred in The Great Society on Broadway, as Stokely Carmichael. A sequel to Robert Schenkkan’s Tony-winning All The Way, The Great Society follows President Lyndon B. Johnson and chronicles the high-water mark of the programs of the Great Society, and the growing tragedy in Vietnam. Before hitting the Broadway stage, Marchánt starred in the Chris Morris india film This Day Shall Come, which was a social satire also starring Anna Kendrick. In the movie, he played ‘Moses,’ an impoverished Haitian preacher who is offered cash to save his family from eviction. He has no idea his sponsor works for the FBI who plan to turn him into a criminal by fueling his madcap revolutionary dreams. 

Early in 2019, Marchánt was in Jordan E. Cooper’s Ain’t No Mo’ Off-Broadway production, which was met with rave reviews and several A-List viewers through producer Lee Daniels.
