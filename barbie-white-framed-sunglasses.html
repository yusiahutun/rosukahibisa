Jenna Coleman wears white framed Barbie sunglasses at fashion week

Publish date: 2024-08-21

While the catwalk collections are the star attraction at fashion week, the front-row fashion is always competing for our attention – with the likes of Cardi B making a dramatic entrance at Schiaparelli, Natalie Portman looking classically elegant at Dior and Naomi Campbell turning heads at Alaïa. As the style set arrived to take their seats at Chanel, one accessory in particular stood out: Jenna Coleman's Barbie-esque white framed sunglasses.

The actress was seen arriving at the show along the river Seine, pairing her statement specs with a blush pink outfit – consisting of a matching striped long-sleeve top and skirt, a pink Chanel handbag and a pair of the brand's classic two-tone slingback pumps – a look that wouldn't have looked out of place in Margot Robbie's much-talked-about Barbie tour wardrobe.

Marc Piasecki//Getty Images

With Greta Gerwig's live-action adaptation hitting UK cinemas on 21 July, Barbie fever has taken over the fashion industry – and Robbie has clearly been having the time of her life paying sartorial tribute to the iconic doll. From vintage Versace and Moschino to Bottega Veneta, Valentino and, of course, Chanel, Robbie's stylist Andrew Mukamal has worked with the fashion houses to pull together a fabulously kitsch and playful wardrobe inspired by Barbie looks from the '50s and '60s through to modern day – with a recurring accessory being those white framed sunglasses.

James Gourley//Getty Images

Margot Robbie promoting Barbie

The accessory has been popular for decades (they were, after all, worn by the first ever issued Barbie in 1959), and is regularly worn by the most fabulous and fashionable – from Bella Hadid to Rihanna, Marion Cotillard to Heidi Klum – striking the perfect balance between statement-making and classic. This is one style that will definitely never date.

TheStewartofNY//Getty Images

MEGA//Getty Images

Jacopo Raule//Getty Images

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Whether you're a lover of Coleman's Chanel shades, prefer something sporty like Hadid or bigger frames like Rihanna, there are plenty of styles to invest in now, no matter your personal aesthetic. One thing is for sure though, we guarantee you'll be wearing yours for years, as Barbie herself has been.
