Simon Keys' biography

Simon Keys is a television personality, conservationist, snake catcher, and tattoo artist. People best know him from Snake City on Nat Geo.

His unusual career path has caught the attention of numerous people globally. The heavily tattooed television personality has British nationality, and his ethnicity is White.

Where was Simon Keys born?

The reptile conservationist was born in England. He spent most of his childhood in Hertfordshire, where his love for nature and reptiles grew.

He discovered his first newt at the age of five, which he found exciting. Although his friends and family were terrified by reptiles, he did not keep away from them. Instead, he explored more about reptiles, amphibians, and fish.

He would go out searching for these animals. He also read every book he could find on reptiles, and at 12, he convinced his parents to allow him to have a pet corn snake.

Career

The television personality started his career as a reptile conservationist. By 30, he owned the largest exotic and venomous snake business in England. He kept different species ranging from taipans and vipers to kraits and rattlesnakes.

In 2005, he relocated to Durban, South Africa, after getting married. In South Africa, he opened a wildlife rescue company. His firm rescued and removed reptiles and animals from people's homes and properties. He would then release them back into the wild.
