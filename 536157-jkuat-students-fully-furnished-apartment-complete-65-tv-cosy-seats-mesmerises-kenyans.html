JKUAT Student's Fully-Furnished Apartment, Complete with 65-Inch TV, Cosy Seats Mesmerises Kenyans

Publish date: 2024-03-03
  • A video showcasing the fully furnished apartment of a Kenyan university student has gone viral on social media, sparking admiration and amazement
  • The student hails from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) and has impressed many with the stylish interior of his apartment
  • The clip highlighted the apartment's cosy recliner seats, a large wall-mounted television, and other expensive appliances

A video showing a fully furnished apartment belonging to a Kenyan university student has gone viral on social media and left many in awe.

The student from the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology mesmerised many on social media as the clip showed his neatly organised apartment furnished with top-notch furniture.

In the clip, the beautiful apartment's cosy recliner seats and huge television set mounted on the wall stole the attention of Kenyans, with many asking for the secret to having such an opulent-looking apartment.
