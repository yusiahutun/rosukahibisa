'Shameful' mass brawl inside Aintree Racecourse as fists fly in shocking footage

Publish date: 2024-05-26

A shocking video has captured the moment a massive fight broke out at Aintree Racecourse as "shameful" blokes swung punches at each other.

In the video captured on Saturday, December 9, during Becher Chase day at the famous racecourse, multiple men were seen fighting while G4S security guards watched. One man can be seen throwing punches at another who is held in a headlock by a second person.

Another man appears to step in to break up the scrap but then gets walloped by one of the two men who starts lashing out at the guy. The fight even spills from the stand into the stairwell in the clip, which has gained more than a million views on social media.
