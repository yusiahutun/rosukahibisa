Half braids, half sew-in hairstyles that will look good on you

Publish date: 2024-04-11

Looking for a new hairdo can be a tiring process, especially if you are still determining what you want. However, with the correct information, you can easily find the look that suits you. One design you should consider is the half braids, half sew-in hairstyle. They offer a unique blend of tradition and trendiness, making it a versatile choice for various occasions.

A transition to a new hairstyle can be challenging, but it can also be an exciting journey of self-discovery and transformation. One such transformative style is the half braids, half sew-in hairstyle. This style is not just a fashion statement, but it’s also a celebration of individuality and creativity.

Best half braids, half sew-in hairstyles to try

What are half braids and half sew-in hairstyles? It is a unique style that combines the edginess of braids with the versatility of sew-ins. On one half, you have beautifully crafted braids, and on the other, you have a sew-in that allows you to experiment. Here are half-braids, half-sew-in hairstyles for black hair to try.

1. Half weave half-braids

The half-wave half-braids hairstyle is a creative and versatile look that combines two different hair techniques to create a unique blend. In this style, the stylist has divided the hair into two sections: one section is braided, while the other section has a weave or hair extensions.

2. Half-up half-down feed in braids with weave

This style is made by parting the hair into two sections from ear to ear, then plaiting feed in cornrow braids into an updo at the top section. The stylist has attached a weave at the back of the head to give the hair more volume.

3. Half braids, half quick weave

This style is made by parting the hair into two sections and then plaiting quick weaves into each section. You can choose any quick weave you like, such as microfiber, silk, or lace. This style is easy to maintain and gives you a natural-looking result.

4. Half braids, half Bantu knots

To achieve this style, the stylist parts the hair into two sections from one side to another, then plaiting bantu knots into each section. This style is fun and playful, and it works well for curly or oily hair.

5. Half feed-in braids, half sew-in

The half-feed-in braids and half-sew-in hairstyle is a creative and versatile look that combines two different hair techniques to create a unique blend. In this style, the stylist divides the hair into two sections: one section with braids and the other with a weave or hair extension.

6. Half & Half quick weave

The half-and-half quick weave is a versatile hairstyle that allows for a mix of creativity and convenience. This style combines natural hair with weave extensions, giving you the best of both worlds. The ‘half and half’ refers to the method of leaving half of your natural hair out while the other half is covered with the weave.

7. Half stitch, half crochet

A half stitch half crochet involves weaving hair extensions into braids that are half crocheted and half sewn. This unique blend of techniques allows for a versatile and dynamic look, offering the best of both worlds.

8. Half weave and half stitch braids

The half-weave and half-stitch braids are versatile hairstyles suitable for any occasion. This unique blend of weaving and stitching techniques creates a distinctive pattern that stands out in a crowd. The style is not only aesthetically pleasing but also practical, as it helps protect the hair from damage and promotes growth.

9. Half-braids half-sew in straight hair

The half-braid, half-sew-in straight hair combines two different types of braids: a half-braid and a sew-in weave, but with straight hair. The half-braid starts from the scalp and goes halfway down the hair, creating a natural-looking part, while the sew-in weave is a hair extension that is attached to the scalp with glue or clips.

10. Half-braids, half sew-in curly hair

If you have curly hair and think that this half-braid style is not possible, then you are in for a pleasant surprise. The half-braid, half-sew-in for curly hair, is a breathtaking design ideal for soft or curly hair.

11. Half-stitch braids and half sewn

The half stitch braids and half sew-in is a versatile hairstyle that offers a unique blend of tradition and innovation. This style involves plaiting half of the hair in intricate stitch braids while the other half is adorned with a sew-in weave.

12. Zig zag half stich & half sew-in

The zig-zag half stitch and half sew-in is a unique hairstyle that embodies both creativity and style. This look involves creating zig-zag patterned plaits on one half of the head while the other half features a sew-in weave. The zig-zag braids add a fun and edgy element to the hairstyle.

13. Half-braids, half sew-in hairstyles with beads

If you are looking for half-braids and half-sewed hairstyles with beads, the tribal stitch with tribal hair is an ideal pick. This hairstyle involves plaiting half of the head with neat cornrows and the rest of the hair with sewed-in braids. You can accessorise the ends of the braiding with beads for a natural look.

14. Half-braids half-sew in hairstyles with weave

If you are looking for a trendy and unique hairstyle design, the half-braid, half-sew-in is an ideal choice. The method comprises half of the head plaited with cornrows while the other half is an extension.

15. Half install, half-braids

The half-install combines two different types of braids to achieve a unique and attractive combo design. This particular design has utilised trendy thin braiding at the top of the hair while adding an extension to the back of the head.

16. Half up, half down

As the name suggests, this hairstyle utilised space to achieve a unique design. The stylist has plaited one-half of the head with cornrows that converge into a ponytail hairstyle, where they have added an extension.
