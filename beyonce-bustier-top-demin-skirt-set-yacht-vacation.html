See Beyonc's Corset Top Bustier Top and Matching Denim Jacket and Skirt

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Beyoncé's 40th birthday may have passed two weeks ago, but she is still celebrating.

The superstar shared new posts from a dinner party, and showed of the night's look, during her ongoing birthday cruise on Instagram yesterday. In the series of posts, the "Black Parade" singer paired a white bustier top with a matching denim jacket and wrap skirt printed with an animal and tree pattern. She also wore white block-heel sandals, a statement diamond necklace, chandelier earrings, and a pair of small white sunglasses.

Her husband Jay-Z also featured in several of the pics, including one where the dinner party raised their glasses to cheers, and another where he showed his own dinner fit. The rapper chose a tropical orange shirt printed with Playboy bunnies, black pants, and gray sneakers.

The "Crazy in Love" singer has filled her timeline with snaps from her European vacation, during which she and her family have sailed through Italy and the South of France on a 400-foot megayacht. The star's outfits have ranged from polished casual to full glam, including a Rowen Rose romper, a David Koma minidress, and a head-to-toe Valentino look.

The Ivy Park founder even missed the year's biggest red carpet at the Met Gala while on her trip. Luckily, she still shared a gorgeous look Wednesday night to make up for her absence. In the pics, she donned a monochromatic outfit, made up of a pastel green Balmain mini dress with gold detailing, paired with a jacket and Louboutins. She accessorized the look with a gold rectangular Oscar de la Renta bag, Lorraine Schwartz earrings, nighttime sunglasses, and a bold red lip.
