Eva Mendes models new spring collection for NY &amp; Company

Publish date: 2024-09-28

Eva Mendes is the first to model her new range for New York & Co after recently coming out of her red-carpet retirement to debut the fashion line.

The actress made her first appearance on the red carpet in six months to launch her clothing designs for the high-street brand in their Miami store last week.

The Place Beyond the Pines star – who is ambassador for New York & Co and co-designed the collection – modelled pieces from the range including a strapless prom dress and a summery coral-coloured silk frock.

Speaking about the collection previously, Mendes had told WWD: "The other day, I went to the office with half my closet.

"Part of the fun is coming up with the designs and looking through my closet. There are a lot of prints and lots of colour. Black can be very chic and very New York."

At the launch of the range last Friday, Mendes opened up about getting back into shape after welcoming her second daughter with her partner Ryan Gosling.

NY & Company Instagram

"In some ways, it's been more challenging to get back in shape after my second daughter," she told reporters (via the MailOnline).

"Yet it's not as hard as I thought it would be, because I'm always running around with the kids. I never sit down – I'm on the move all day."

She's previously spoken about preferring to stay at home with her two girls than gracing the red carpet, telling Shape magazine: "What people don't know about me is that I love being home. Instead of hitting the red carpet, I'd rather be with our girls."

Mendes and Gosling have two-year-old Esmeralda and 11-month-old Amada.
