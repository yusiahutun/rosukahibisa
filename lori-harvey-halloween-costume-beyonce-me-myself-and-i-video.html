See Lori Harvey Recreate Beyonc's &quot;Me, Myself, and I&quot; Video for Halloween

Publish date: 2024-06-04

Lori Harvey channeled Queen Bey's early years for her Halloween look.

The model recreated two looks from Beyoncé's music video for "Me, Myself, and I," one of the many chart-topping singles from the singer's 2003 solo album Dangerously in Love. Harvey looks just like the star in her two posts, which put images and scenes from the video side-by-side with her own costumes.

For the first outfit, the SKN by Lori Harvey founder donned a honey blonde wig with blunt bangs as she posed in an off-the-shoulder bodycon dress, finishing the look with an oh-so-'00s nude lip. She also filmed the scene from the video, crawling around a black room with a pink settee and gold lamp in a perfect recreation.

Harvey captioned the pic with the first line from the single-woman anthem, "All the ladies if you feel me help me sing it outttt."

The model also filmed the video's parking garage scene, in a clip showing her walking backwards just like the original reversed clip. Her second costume was an identical lime green and white colorblock gown as in the video, with a plunging neckline, tie-front detail, thigh-high slit, hip cutouts, and a diamond-encrusted belt.

She finished the look with similar ornate hoop earrings, white opera gloves, a white fur stole, a blonde wig with bangs, and matching makeup, while carrying a black bag and a pair of keys (though she didn't film the car keying scene).

Harvey is currently single herself, having announced her split from Creed actor Michael B. Jordan last June after a year and a half of dating. In her August appearance on Bumble Presents Luv2SeeIt With Teyana Taylor, she shared her biggest red flag for dating.

"If you are too close to your exes, that’s a red flag. Cordial? Okay. I don’t want y’all to have beef either because that gets messy," she said, adding that her green flags are transparency and communication.
