Mike Love's children

How many kids does Mike Love have? He has been blessed with many children. He welcomed his children during his early days as a co-founder of The Beach Boys and later in life. Some of his children have different mothers. Here are his children:

Teresa Love is Mike Love’s second-born daughter. She was born on 2 December 1962 in California, United States. Her mother’s name is Francie St. Martin.

Shawn Marie Love

After separating from his first wife in 1963, Mike Love had an affair with Shannon Ann Harris. Shannon worked as a secretary for Mike. She eventually got pregnant and welcomed Shawn Marie Love Harris Wilson on December 30, 1964, in Los Angeles County, California.

Despite their romance, Mike and Shannon never married. Furthermore, Mike Love refused to accept paternity, forcing Shannon to file a paternity suit against him in March 1965.

The suit was settled out of court, but Mike never expressively accepted paternity. He paid child support and agreed to allow Shawn to use his surname, Love. Unfortunately, Shawn Marie Love Harris Wilson died of liver problems at age 38 in September 2003.

After learning about the pregnancy, Mike wanted to take Frances to Mexico for a feticide. However, Francie’s mother went to Mike’s parents and arranged a shotgun marriage.

Consequently, Mike Love’s mother threw him out of her house by tossing his belongings from the second-story balcony. A year later, Mike Love and Francie St. Martin welcomed a second daughter, Teresa. Unfortunately, the marriage between Mike Love and Francie ended in 1963.

The couple finalised their divorce on 22 May 1964. Financial troubles and an abusive Mike were some of the things cited for the divorce.
