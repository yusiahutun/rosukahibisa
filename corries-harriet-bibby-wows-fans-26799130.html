Corrie's Harriet Bibby wows fans in stunning two-piece as she celebrates birthday with co-stars

Publish date: 2024-03-02

Coronation Street star Harriet Bibby looked stunning as she shared a string of pictures of herself on social media celebrating her birthday with co-stars.

The newly turned 24-year-old looked gorgeous in a matching floral patterned skirt and crop top with chunky beige boots that flaunted her youthful style on Instagram.

The actress celebrated with her co-stars Elle Mulvaney, who plays her best friend Amy Barlow, and Jack James Ryan who plays Amy's boyfriend Jacob Hay in Corrie.

They were also joined by Harriet's sister and fellow actor Emily Wynne as they tagged in 42nd Street Nightclub and Revolution at Deansgate Locks, Manchester.

Harriet Bibby took over the role of Summer Spellman from Matilda Freeman back in 2020.

Last week on the show, Summer was forced to confront her secret eating disorder after Amy raised concerns with Billy.

Corrie fans have seen Summer overwhelmed with stress revising for her exams while battling with bulimia and managing her type one diabetes.

A worried Amy recently caught Summer throwing up after eating and she told Billy. Billy has since been keeping an eye on Summer and in Friday night's episode he told her he'd arranged for Summer to attend a diabetes support group and that her revision could wait.

Summer reluctantly went along to the group and met Aaron, a fellow diabetic. Fans were left wondering if this could be the start of a new romance for Summer and the friendly newcomer.
