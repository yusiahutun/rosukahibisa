Taylor Swift Without Makeup — She ‘Woke Up Like This’

“I woke up like thissss. (With a cat on me),” captioned the singer as her equally cute cat, Olivia Benson, snuggled with the singer in a photo from March 5.

But really, how does the pop star roll out of bed looking naturally fierce and flawless a la Beyonce?

Well, as we previously reported, Taylor gets her glow by making sure to always go to bed without an ounce of makeup!

“Washing your face is very important. Also, moisturizing. Everybody goes on and on about that. I struggle to make myself take off my makeup,” revealed Taylor in the November issue of InStyle.

Get Taylor Swift’s Squeaky Clean Skin

Although the star continues to be inspired by her elaborate evening looks for events and performances, Taylor explained to InStyle that cleaning her face each night is essential to her regimen.

“‘You know when you’re like wow, I just got done with a photo shoot and I had this amazing makeup artist put makeup on me. I wish I could look like this tomorrow.’ I have to talk myself into taking my makeup off. ‘You cannot just trace it back on tomorrow, that’s gross,” added the singer.

In order to shake off, or rather wash off, your makeup, we recommend a cleanser that has become a cult favorite among beauty gurus: the Fresh Soy Face Cleanser.

The amino acid-rich cleanser promotes moisture retention, elasticity and firmness, while the calming cucumber extract soothes skin.

Lastly, if you’re fair skined like Ms. Swift, it’s important to protect your porcelain skin with sun screen.

Be it in the Big Apple or in Tinseltown, La Roche-Posay Anthelios Tinted Mineral Ultra Light Body Sunscreen will help Taylor look camera ready while also making sure that her skin stays forever young!
