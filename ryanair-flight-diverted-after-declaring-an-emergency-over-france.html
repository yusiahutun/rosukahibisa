Ryanair flight diverted due to sick passenger lands safely

Publish date: 2024-03-12

A Ryanair flight  was diverted to land in Paris, Airlive.net has reported.

Flight FR1633 from Limoges to the East Midlands was diverted to Paris Beauvais where it landed safely.

A Ryanair spokesman said: "This flight from Limoges to East Midlands (27 Sept) diverted to Paris Beauvais after a customer became ill on board.

"The aircraft landed normally and the customer was met by medical personnel, and disembarked for further treatment.

"The aircraft is due to depart for East Midlands shortly. Ryanair sincerely apologised to affected customers for any inconvenience caused.”
