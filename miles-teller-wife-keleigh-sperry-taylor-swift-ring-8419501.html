Keleigh Sperry, Miles Teller's Wife, Revealed That She Gifted Taylor Swift Her Huge Ring

Publish date: 2024-03-27

Over the weekend, Swifties everywhere were convinced that Taylor Swift had gotten a very special piece of jewelry from her beau Travis Kelce — with some even going as far as to believe that it was an engagement ring. However, today, Keleigh Sperry, who is married to fellow actor Miles Teller and very much in Swift's squad, let her Instagram followers know that the ring was from her.

Across a close-up shot of the pear-shaped ring were the words, "Love this ring I had made for my friend, she’s bejeweled.” Not only did she quote a lyric from one of Swift's most beloved songs, but Sperry managed to set the record straight after several outlets pushed the engagement rumors.

The photos in question were taken on Dec. 13, on the night of Swift’s 34th birthday. In a few of the snaps from the festivities, Swift can be seen with the new ring, which features an oversized opal gemstone surrounded by a halo of blue topaz crystals. While paparazzi managed to get images of Swift coming and going from her party — which featured stars like Blake Lively and Zoë Kravitz — it was her friends (and the songstress herself) who offered a peek at what happened inside behind closed doors.

"Can’t believe this year … actually … happened? Thank you for all your beautiful birthday wishes yesterday," Swift wrote alongside a carousel of photos that included cameos from Gigi Hadid, Abigail Anderson, Jack Antonoff, Sabrina Carpenter, Gracie Abrams, Este Haim, Alana Haim, Antoni Porowski, and more.
