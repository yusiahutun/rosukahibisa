Paris Hilton rumoured to be dating Australian model Jordan Barrett

Publish date: 2024-04-11

    Paris Hilton has sparked speculation that she has a new boyfriend after sharing Instagram pictures of herself partying with Australian model Jordan Barrett on Sunday. The duo, who are both in Milan for Fashion Week, looked incredibly close in the pictures taken at Just Cavalli club. "#GoodTimes at @JustCavalliMilano with @IblameJordan" wrote Paris, alongside a photo of herself posing in sunglasses while Jordan stands with his arm draped over her shoulder.

    Paris Hilton posted a picture of herself with model Jordan Barrett on Instagram

    Jordan shared the same picture to his own Instagram account, writing "Did you say round II @ParisHilton & All again @robertcavalli #MFW". The 18-year-old also posted another picture which showed Paris smiling as she cuddles up to him. "Oh dear…" Jordan captioned the picture.

    Jordan also shared a picture of them together at Just Cavalli in Milan

    The rumoured couple were also seen partying in the club's DJ booth together. Paris was the DJ for Just Cavalli's party in honour of Milan Fashion Week, and dubbed herself the "Party Princess in Milan" in an earlier Instagram post. Heiress Paris split from her boyfriend of two years, 23-year-old River Viiperi, in July. After the split Paris was linked to Ralph Lauren model Josh Upshaw when the pair were pictured kissing on a beach in Malibu. Paris, who is 15 years older than her rumoured new love interest, has previously spoken about how dating a younger man "isn't a big deal".
