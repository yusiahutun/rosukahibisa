Sara Bareilles Announces Much Anticipated Amidst The Chaos Tour

Publish date: 2024-03-27

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Grammy, Tony and Emmy award-nominated singer, songwriter, actress, and author Sara Bareilles has today (April 8) announced her Amidst The Chaos Tour. 

The 30-city trek will kick off October 5th in Detroit, MI and make stops in New York City, Boston, Chicago, Houston, Los Angeles, San Francisco, and more, before wrapping November 26th in Atlanta, GA.

The tour takes Bareilles on the road in support of her fifth full-length album Amidst The Chaos, which was released last Friday to rave reviews. This marks her first album of original material since 2013.

Tickets go on sale to the general public beginning Friday, April 12th at 10:00AM local time at LiveNation.com.
