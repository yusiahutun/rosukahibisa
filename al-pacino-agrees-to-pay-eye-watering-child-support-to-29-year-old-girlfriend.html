Al Pacino agrees to pay eye-watering child support to 29-year-old girlfriend

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Months after welcoming a child with Noor Alfallah, who is 54 years his junior, Al Pacino has agreed to pay a hefty amount for child support.

Al Pacino became one of Hollywood‘s oldest fathers after his girlfriend, Noor Alfallah, gave birth to a baby boy named Roman on June 15, 2023. At 83 years old, The Godfather actor has a 54-year age gap with 29-year-old Alfallah. The couple has now signed a custody agreement which states Pacino has agreed to fork out a five-figure sum for child support.

Al Pacino to pay over $30K in child support to Noor Alfallah

According to documents obtained by TMZ, Noor will receive primary physical custody of their son, while the actor – real name Alfredo James Pacino – will be granted visitation rights. The celebrity couple will negotiate on holidays and make joint decisions for Roman’s education.

The Oscar-winning star has agreed to pay at least $30K per month in payments to his girlfriend. That figure, however, is likely to increase in accordance with his income.

That’s not the only expense; Al will be paying $110K upfront and another $15K annually for an education fund when Roman is older. TMZ also claims the star could pay up to an additional $90K by the end of 2023, which is determined by his income.

Alfallah and Pacino first made headlines in April 2022, with speculation they met on his film, Billy Knight, where she worked as executive producer. Noor’s pregnancy was confirmed one month before giving birth and despite the custody agreement fueled break-up rumors, Pacino’s representative told US Weekly that they are still together.

Al Pacino is a father of four

Before Roman’s arrival, The Irishman was a father of three children from two previous relationships. He has never been married.

His eldest daughter, Julie Marie, was born October 16, 1989, whom he shares with ex-girlfriend and acting coach Jan Tarrant. She followed her parents’ footsteps into the movie industry, becoming a producer and director.

Marie gained two siblings in 2001 when Pacino and actress Beverly D’Angelo welcomed twins, son Anton James and daughter Olivia Rose. The couple were in a relationship from 1997 until 2003.

Despite his fame, the Scarface actor has kept his kids largely private. Anton is not active on social media, but Olivia boasts nearly 20K Instagram followers.
