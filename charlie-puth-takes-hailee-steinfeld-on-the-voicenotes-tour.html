Charlie Puth Takes Hailee Steinfeld On The Voicenotes Tour

Publish date: 2024-07-11

LOS ANGELES, California (CelebrityAccess) – Yesterday multi-platinum recording artist Charlie Puth announced The Voicenotes Tour to accompany the release of his upcoming album “VOICENOTES.” Tickets go on sale this week via Live Nation and the tour will kick off July 11 in Toronto. Rising star Hailee Steinfeld will be the primary opening act for all shows on the tour.

Charlie Puth is currently promoting his new single, “How Long,” which has almost 300 million worldwide streams topping a variety of iTunes Global Singles charts and receiving gold certification in Australia. The song’s video, shown below, has reached over 168 million views. Vanity Fair has named it one of “The 17 Best Pop Songs of 2017.”

More streams and views can be expected as Puth is in the midst of numerous tv appearances including tonight’s finale episode of The Voice, Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve 2018 and a January appearance on the Ellen DeGeneres Show.

“Charlie Puth – ‘How Long’ [Official Video]”

Hailee Steinfeld, a multi-platinum artist in her own right, will be the primary opening act for the tour. She’s been gathering up awards in 2017 including receiving the “Crossover Artist” award from Variety, “Top Covered Artist” by Musical.ly at the 2017 Billboard Music Awards and “Best Push Artist” at the MTV EMAs.
