Love Islands India quits topless modelling amid Ovie romance

Publish date: 2024-07-10

The stunning model finished in third place alongside her villa partner Ovie Soko on last night’s Love Island.

And it appears as though things are getting serious for India Reynolds, 28, and her man.

Last night’s jaw-dropping finale saw Amber Gill and Greg O’Shea crowned the winners of the show.

But it looks as though the real winners are India and Ovie, who appear to more smitten than ever.

Speaking to Daily Star Online today ahead of their return to UK, the pair revealed they plan on keeping their romance going.

Ovie explained: “We are just going to continue, we have set a pretty good foundation and we’ve spoken a lot about it.

“In today’s society, everything moves a million miles an hour but we’ve had two weeks to get to know each other on a more intimate level as far as us being face to face all the time.”

Meanwhile, India gushed over her hunky beau while dismissing rumours that she had set her sights on him in a bid to win the show.

She said: “I went for Ovie because we had such a good connection from the start. We’ve spoken about it a lot and as long as we are okay, other people’s opinions won’t affect us.”

The stunner also made a bombshell revelation regarding her career.

It’s no secret that the India was previously a glamour model, having appeared semi-naked for the likes of Page Three, Nuts, FHM, Loaded and Zoo.

She has also stipped off to her undies for the UK's biggest sex toy firm, Lovehoney.

However, India revealed here former racy topless shoots are now firmly behind her.

She explained: “I wasn’t a glamour model when I went into the villa.

“I gave it up five years ago before I even went in here [into the villa].”

The beauty added that while she won’t be whipping her kit off anytime soon, she will be going back into commercial modelling.
