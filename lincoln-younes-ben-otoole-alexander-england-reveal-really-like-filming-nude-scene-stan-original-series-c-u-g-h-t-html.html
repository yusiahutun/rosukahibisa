Lincoln Younes, Ben O'Toole and Alexander England reveal what it was really like filming a nude scen

Publish date: 2024-02-20

Lincoln Younes, Ben O'Toole and Alexander England have opened up about filming a nude scene together for Stan Original series C*A*U*G*H*T.

During the fist episode of the new series, the trio and co-star Kick Gurry are piled up on top of one another naked in a ditch after they try to escape terrorists.

Lincoln told Daily Mail Australia at Wednesday night's premiere: 'It was liberating but very weird especially because it's with your best friends and one of them wrote the scene.'

'We shot it three times... I've had words with my friends since then and said let's not do it again,' he jokingly added.

Ben also explained that the scene was filmed over two full days, before joking: 'It was easily Alex's favourite.'

The Stan Original series C*A*U*G*H*T is available now only on Stan.

'We were filming it for way too long,' Alex added.

Alex also revealed the nude scene was his one of his favourite scenes to film from the series.

'I loved the nude scene because it was us all as friends in this really compromising situation, all making each other laugh,' he explained.

'There was total trust there. It should be a very vulnerable position but we were all just having a great time and that's the spirit of the show.'

Ben continued: 'We are all willing to make ourselves look like idiots and we are all willing to take risks and that scene is a microcosm of the whole series.'

The plot concerns four Australian soldiers getting captured by freedom fighters after being mistaken for Americans and being forced to produce a hostage video that goes viral.

When the soldiers reach celebrity status, they realise that being caught might just be the best thing that could have happened to them.
