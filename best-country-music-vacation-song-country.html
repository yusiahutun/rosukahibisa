What's Country's Best Vacation Song?

Publish date: 2024-02-07

Evan Paul is the host of Taste of Country Nights, a syndicated radio show heard on more than 100 country radio stations nationwide. Producer Amber co-hosts the show with him every night from 7PM to midnight. Together they play the best new country music and interview today's top stars, like Brantley Gilbert, Justin Moore, Maren Morris + more! 

Happy Labor Day to you and your family! We know the last place you want to be or think about today is your job so, in honor of the holiday, we want to know what are some of your favorite country songs about being on vacation?

For me, it's Alan Jackson and Jimmy Buffet "It's Five O'Clock Somewhere." For Amber, it's Dierks Bentley's "Somewhere on a Beach."

Help us celebrate Labor Day by adding your country vacation song to the list!
