American comedian Joy Behar plans to leave the daytime talk show The View in the summer of 2022. Learn more about her through this article.

Josephine Victoria, aka Joy Behar, is an American comedian, TV host, writer, as well as and actress. She was born in 1942 and is mainly known for co-hosting the ABC daytime talk show The View.

Joy has hosted many shows, including The Joy Behar Show and Joy Behar: Say Anything! Her most recent weekly late-night talk show was Late Night Joy, which aired on TLC in 2015.

Moreover, the talk show host is originally from Williamsburg, Brooklyn. She is the only child of a Roman Catholic family. Behar has a BA degree in sociology from Queens College. She even studied acting at the HB Studio.
