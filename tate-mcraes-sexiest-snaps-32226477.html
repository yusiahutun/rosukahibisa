Tate McRae's sexiest snaps racy lingerie, plunging bikinis and eye-popping display

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Tate McRae is set to perform at the BRIT Awards and is also a nominee, so to mark her big night we have a look at some of the sexiest shots she's shared with fans

Tate McRae has been announced as a performer at this weekend's BRIT Awards, and she is expected to pull out all the stops.

The Canadian singer-songwriter has been nominated for International Song of the Year for Greedy, and she has also joined the likes of Calvin Harris and Ellie Goulding, Dua Lipa and Kylie Minogue on the bill for the glitziest event in the music industry's calendar. Calling it the "biggest honour", she has promised her fans that it is "going to be an unforgettable night".

Whatever she sings, Tate is likely to turn up the heat. The star may have found fame for her singing and dancing, but she's also cast a spell on thousands of people with her stunning looks and sizzling Instagram uploads.

To mark her debut on the BRITS stage, we take a look at some of the Run For The Hills singer's hottest ever snaps.

The BRITs take place on 2 March at The O2 Arena in London.
