Taylor Swift stopped touching her male dancers chest and Travis Kelce must be the reason

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Fans have noticed that Taylor Swift stopped touching one of her male dancers on the chest during The Eras Tour and think they know why.

The Anti-Hero singer has a very strict routine for every song that she sticks to, but it looks like an alteration was made for a very special someone.

Taylor Swift stops touching dancer’s chest

Ever since The Eras Tour began, Taylor Swift ran her finger down a very dashing dark-haired male dancer’s chest.

The move came in the routine for her track Lavender Haze and coincided with the line: “Get it off your chest.”

She did it during every single performance, starting back in March when the tour kicked off in Glendale, Arizona.

Taylor wears a glittery lilac dress and fur coat during the track, while her dancer wears a white shirt that shows off his abs.

One day she suddenly stopped touching the man’s chest seductively and did the hand motion without contact instead.

Fans didn’t really notice initially, but it soon became obvious that she’d stopped doing the touching for good.

The reason why is totally obvious

Looking back, is seems like the routine change came because there was a new guy on the scene – Travis Kelce!

Fans say she stopped touching his chest in around August, which is when the pair were first romantically linked.

The NFL star attended Taylor’s tour at the end of July and then decided to shoot his shot at the singer on his podcast.

By September, it was pretty obvious that something was going on between the pair – and the rest is history!

A Twitter/X post has gone viral asking when Taylor stopped doing the gesture and one fan commented: “It was definitely when she got with Travis.”

“When her and Travis started dating,” another agreed while a third also wrote: “I think we all know when.”

Travis is the only man Taylor’s touching!
