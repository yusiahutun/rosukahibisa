Best Red for Darker Skin

Chanel Le Vernis Longwear Nail Color in Gitane

Best Red for Darker Skin

Chanel Le Vernis Longwear Nail Color in Gitane

Credit: Bloomingdale's

Chanel's pigmented Gitane red is a standout on darker skintones, and formulated with conditioning ceramides to help keep your nails healthy and strong. "The degree of brightness and the tone of the nail polish is what makes one shade stand out from another within the context of how the red polish contrasts with the user's skin tone," Choi adds. "Brightness refers to how light or dark the red shade is and tone refers to the opacity and depth of the pigmentation and prevailing undertone. The higher the pigmentation, the more intense the color."

Best Red for Fair Skin

Christian Louboutin Rouge Louboutin Nail Colour

Best Red for Fair Skin

Christian Louboutin Rouge Louboutin Nail Colour

Credit: Neiman Marcus

Professional nail artists say that red polishes featuring bluer or cooler undertones are best for fairer skin tones. This pick is inspired by the shade of Christian Louboutin's coveted soles.
