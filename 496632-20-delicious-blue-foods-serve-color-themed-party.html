20 delicious blue foods to serve at your colour-themed party

Publish date: 2024-02-18

Are you planning a colour-themed party and looking for ways to incorporate the hue of blue into your menu? Naturally, blue foods may be hard to find, but that doesn't mean you can't enjoy delicious blue foods. Whether it is a blue-themed birthday or baby shower, you can still impress your guests with a stunning array of blue dishes that look amazing and taste even better.

Serving delicious blue treats at your colour-themed party can wow your guests and make them return for more. The blue foods category has something for everyone, from sweet treats like blue velvet cupcakes to savoury snacks like blue corn chips with guacamole. So with just a little creativity, indulge and tantalise your guests' taste buds with eye-catching, mouth-watering blue dishes.

Delicious blue foods for your colour-themed party

The blue colour is often associated with calmness, tranquillity, and peace. However, blue is less common when it comes to food, which makes it more intriguing. But there are a variety of blue foods and snacks. Is any food naturally blue? Although not common, naturally blue foods come in various shades, from light blue to dark bluish purple.

1. Blue corn tortillas

Blue corn tortillas will perfectly add colour and flavour to your party table. These vibrant tortillas are made from blue corn, which gives them a slightly sweeter and nuttier taste than traditional corn tortillas. They are a great way to add a unique twist to your party tacos, quesadillas, and nachos.

They also make a colourful appetiser like blue corn bruschetta or blue corn chips and dip. So whether you're hosting a casual backyard party or a sophisticated dinner party, blue corn tortillas are a delicious and memorable menu addition.

2. Blue potatoes salad

Blue potatoes are a colourful twist on traditional potatoes that will impress your party guests. They have a naturally vibrant blue-purple flesh that adds colour to dishes. Serving them in a delicious blue potato salad is impressive.

The blue potatoes are boiled until tender and mixed with a tangy dressing made from mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, and vinegar. The result is a colourful and flavorful side dish perfect for any party. Your guests will love the fun and unexpected addition of blue potatoes to their salad.

3. Blue cheese and bacon stuffed mushrooms

The bite-sized mushrooms are filled with creamy blue cheese, breadcrumbs, crispy bacon, and savoury spices for this savoury and indulgent appetiser. The mushrooms are baked until golden brown and crispy outside but juicy and tender on the inside. The combination creates a deliciously complex irresistible flavour.

These decadent appetisers are perfect for any party, whether a casual get-together or a formal event. They are easy to make ahead of time and can be served hot or at room temperature, making them a versatile and convenient addition to any party menu.

4. Blueberry Balsamic Glazed Pork Tenderloin

This is savoury and sweet blue food for any party guest. The pork tenderloin is seasoned with herbs and then roasted in the oven before being brushed with a glaze made from fresh blueberries and balsamic vinegar. The result is a tender and juicy pork tenderloin with a flavorful and vibrant glaze.

5. Blue tomatoes salsa

Blue tomatoes are packed with antioxidants, and when mixed with finely chopped jalapeno pepper, red onion, and lime juice, they result in a sweet, tangy salsa. The vibrant blue tomatoes add colour to your party table. Serve it with various dippers, such as tortilla chips, pita bread, or sliced veggies, and let your guests enjoy the refreshing, unique salsa.

6. Jenipapo mousse

Jenipapo berries contain genipin, which produces edible blue pigment. Once the juice is extracted from the fruit, with a mixture of blueberries, you can use it to make a blue cocktail or sweet jenipapo ice cream. Jenipapo mousse is made by blending the fruit with cream, sugar, and gelatin and then chilling it until it sets. Jenipapo is versatile for a refreshing summer treat or a blue party dessert.

7. Blue raspberry sorbet

This sorbet, made with fresh blueberries and raspberry puree, has a vibrant blue-purple colour that adds colour to any dessert table. The sweet and tart flavours of the berries combine to create a delicious and fruity taste that is perfect for a summer party.

8. Concord grapes salsa toppings

You can make sweet concord grape salsa or chutney, served as a dip with crackers or as a topping for grilled meats or fish. Another way to incorporate them into your party menu is to make a concord grape cocktail, like a grape martini or a grape sangria.

The deep blue-purple colour of the grapes adds a beautiful touch to any drink. So whether you're looking for a sweet and fruity dessert, a savoury dip, or a festive cocktail, concord grapes are a delicious and colourful ingredient that can create a variety of blue food dishes.

9. Honeyed yoghourt and berry tart with ginger crust

This is a delectable dessert with creamy, sweet, and tangy flavours. The ginger-spiced graham-cracker crust provides a unique and delicious base for the tart, then topped with juicy blueberries to create a beautiful blue hue that's perfect for any occasion.

Best of all, you can prepare this mouth-watering blueberry tart in under an hour, making it a quick and easy dessert to impress your guests. So, add some sweetness and colour to your table with this incredible blue snack.

10. Blue American lobster

This rare lobster has sweet and succulent meat of striking blue colour that you should consider serving. Whether you steam, grill, or bake, this lobster's flavour and beautiful blue hue will make a stunning centrepiece for your table.

You can serve the lobster with melted butter, lemon wedges, blue corn on the cob, and potato salad. Or, consider incorporating the lobster into a blue potato salad, pasta dish, or even a lobster roll. Whatever way you prepare it, Blue American lobster will surely be a showstopper at your blue foods party.

11. Blue Spirulina

This natural blue-green alga has a vibrant blue colour and potential health benefits. The blue spirulina powder can make blue smoothie bowls or blue lattes. It is also used to colour and add a unique flavour to desserts like blue spirulina ice cream and cheesecake.

You can use blue spirulina to make savoury dishes like blue spirulina pasta or blue spirulina sushi. The possibilities are endless with this versatile and nutritious superfood, making it a great addition to any blue food party menu.

12. Texas giant blue fig

The deep blue-purple hue of the fruit's skin and vibrant pink flesh make an eye-catching addition to any plate. This fig has a sweet and slightly tangy flavour, with notes of berry and honey.

It is a versatile party dish that can be enjoyed in sweet and savoury dishes, from fig jams and pastries to salads and charcuterie boards. The fruit is a true delight for any food lover looking to add colour and flavour to their cuisine.

13. Laurestine

Blue foods found in laurestine are visually stunning and offer a range of flavours, from sweet blueberries to tangy blue cheese, adding a pop of colour and flavour to any dish. They can be used in sweet and savoury meals, making them a delicious and vibrant addition to any party food menu.

14. Blueberry macaroons

With a unique and scarce blue colour, macaroons are small, sweet treats in all colours. These blue macaroons, filled with blueberry mascarpone filling, are a class apart and perfect for dessert or as treats at parties. The delicate texture of the shell melts in your mouth, and they are super easy to make.

15. Blue pea flower

Blending the delicate flavour of butterfly pea flowers with condensed milk and vanilla extract is a perfect thirst-quencher with a sweet taste. Serving this stunning blue drink pitcher will keep your guest refilling glasses.

17. Elderberries

Elderberries are an excellent option for adding blue colour to party food. They have a tart flavour and can be used in many dishes, such as jams, syrups, and pies. Elderberry cocktails or as a topping for cakes are good choices. They also go well with cheese on a charcuterie board. 18. The Blue Moon Lime Tart

This delicious dessert boasts a light, delicate texture and a milky, sweet flavour. Every bite topped with blueberries, blackberries, and lime, is infused with a tropical vibe to please your guests. In addition, this recipe requires no baking, making it a perfect option for warmer months' parties.

This is a stunning and delicious dessert perfect for any blue food party. Its layers of blue-coloured cake shaped like a tree, white frosting, and blue sprinkles will impress your guests. This unique, beautiful, tasty dessert adds a magical touch to your party.

20. Starflowers

These beautiful blooms feature vibrant blue petals that make an eye-catching addition to any party's dish. Whether you're using them as a cocktail garnish, salad topper, or dessert decoration, Starflowers will impress your guests with their elegance and mild cucumber flavour.
