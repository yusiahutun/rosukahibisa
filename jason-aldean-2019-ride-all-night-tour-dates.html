Jason Aldean Announces 2019 Ride All Night Tour With Kane Brown

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Jason Aldean just dropped 2019 dates, and he's bringing along some of the hottest young artists in country music.

The superstar revealed the cities and dates for his 2019 Ride All Night Tour on Monday (Jan. 14). Kane Brown and Carly Pearce will accompany Aldean on his 2019 Ride All Night Tour, as will Aldean's good buddy Dee Jay Silver.

"Kane was out with us a couple years ago, and I just kind of hit it off with him. He’s kind of become like my little brother almost," Aldean told reporters at the No. 1 party for his recent hit with Miranda Lambert, "Drowns the Whiskey."

Jason Aldean's Worst Habit? He Spills

"When I’m on tour, I want to be out there with guys I like hanging out with," he added. "And it doesn’t hurt that Kane is on fire right now. He’s about as hot as it gets. Just excited to have him back out, excited to see his show and have a buddy out there for a year."

Aldean also had high praise for Pearce.

"I think it's great to have a female out on the show just to change it up a bit ... so Carly, coming off a big year, she's doing really well, so I think it's gonna be fun. For all three of us, our shows are so different that I think it works out well for those in the show who are going to see it."

The superstar, whose current album is 2018's Rearview Town, named the tour after one of the songs on the record.

"I don’t even really know what the hell it means; it’s just a cool name for the tour," Aldean says. "I’m always excited for the tour; change things up, get a new design for the stage and go out with new stuff. It’s like Christmas morning for us when we get to walk out and have new lighting. It’s like being on a playground for us. It makes the shows fun."
