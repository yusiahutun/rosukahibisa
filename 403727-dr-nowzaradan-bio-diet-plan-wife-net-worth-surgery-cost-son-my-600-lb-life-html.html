My 600-LB Life

My 600-lb Life is a reality show that aired on the TLC TV network. The first episode of the show debuted on 1st February 2012. So far, he has been in eight seasons with over 100 episodes. The show follows the weight loss journey of obese patients seeking treatment from Dr Now.

He helps them lose weight through various forms of surgery performed at his place in Houston, Texas. Besides, he carefully formulates a before and after surgery diet for his patients. Dr Now also helps these patients to acquire psychological help to make the weight loss process effective.

Dr Nowzaradan's diet plan

The doctor has a customized diet plan that suits every patient according to his or her health condition. The diet has a few things in common such as calorie intake restriction to between 1,000 and 1,200 calories per day. Some of the forbidden foods on the diet plan are:

  • Sugar
  • Candy and sweets
  • Chocolate
  • Crackers
  • Potato chips
  • French fries
  • Popcorn
  • Nuts including peanuts
  • White and brown rice
  • Pasta
  • Cereal
  • Fruit juice
  • Bread except in moderation
  • Natural sweeteners like honey

The diet majorly recommends two main components, which are real food and taking small portions. Some of the foods one can take on the diet plan are:

  • Breakfast - Egg whites, turkey sausage, beans, oatmeal, low-fat cottage cheese, plain Greek yoghurt, or whole-grain bread
  • Lunch - Lean protein or canned tuna, deli meat, non-starchy vegetables, and salads without dressing.
  • Dinner - Sagme types of food as lunch. Snacks such as turkey or ham roll-up or plain yoghurt.

Dr Nowzaradan surgery cost

Does Dr Nowzaradan do surgery for free? Yes, he does the surgeries free of charge for all the patients who appear on the reality show. My 600-lb Life covers the costs incurred during the life-saving surgeries. The show covers all the visits to the doctor, the surgeries, and hospital stays.

Additionally, the patients are given some little amount of cash as compensation for appearing on the show. For showing up, each patient receives $1500, and for relocating closer to his clinic, each patient is given a $2500 moving fee.

Dr Nowzaradan's wife and divorce

Is Dr Nowzaradan married? No, he is not. He is currently divorced. He was initially the husband of Delores McRedmond, who worked as a secretary. The duo tied the knot in 1975.

Delores quit her secretarial job to take care of her husband and children. After staying together for 27 years, the duo separated in 2002. She filed for a divorce in 2002, with the court battle taking a whopping two years.
