Career

While growing up, he played improvisational music on pots and pans. Later on, he started practising on real conjunto instruments. Then, with his great expertise, he began performing with gospel bands in his local town.

By 15 years, the singer had secured a gig with one of the local bands. He played at weddings and parties. With a huge desire to reach a broad fan base, he formed a band under the moniker, Siggno in 2000. The music band was made of:
