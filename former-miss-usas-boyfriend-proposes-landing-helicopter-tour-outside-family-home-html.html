A former Miss USA winner got the surprise of her life when a helicopter date with her boyfriend concluded with him landing the chopper in front of her family home, and getting down on one knee.

On Monday, 31-year-old Crystle Stewart, who won the Miss USA pageant in 2008, took off on a helicopter tour of Houston with her Belgian boyfriend Max Sebrechts, a former model, and younger sister Breana.

In a surprise ending, they landed on solid ground right in front of Crystle's family home, where the couple's friends and families were present and cheering as they witnessed Mr Sebrechts pop the question.

The former male model, who now works as a trade commissioner, confessed to KHOU.com: 'My mom came up with the idea actually to rent a helicopter.'

Inviting Crystle's sister along for the ride, he asked her to play an integral role in the proposal - to keep the ring in her pocket.

Things didn't go quite according to plan, however; when the helicopter lands among their group of friends, Breana seemed to forget her duty and walked off.

'Breana had the ring but she gets out and just walks away from the chopper,' Max explains in the video. 'And I'm like, where is she going!'

Slight hiccup: Things didn't go quite according to plan; when the helicopter landed, Crystle's sister Breana, who had the ring in her pocket, walked off

One-of-a-kind date: Max, a former model who now works as a trade commissioner, confessed: 'My mom came up with the idea actually to rent a helicopter'

Crystle's sister backtracks, however, after she realizes her mistake, and the rest of the plan goes off without a hitch.

The video shows Crystle putting her hands over her mouth in surprise as Max gets down on one knee, to the whooping and applauding of the people around them.

He smiles and asks her in his native French to marry him, before sliding the ring onto her finger.

Wedding bells: The couple, who live in Los Angeles, plan to get married in Houston next year

'Aw, you guys!' she exclaims, fanning her face with her hands to hold back tears. 'Let me think about it,' she jokes, causing those around her to break into laughter.

She then shows off her ring to a handful of spectators, who all tell her to 'say yes!' She does.

The couple, who plan to get married in Houston next year, then share a kiss and a warm embrace.

After winning Miss USA, Crystle turned her hand to acting. She now plays Leslie on Tyler Perry's For Better or Worse.'
