Who Is Anastasia Karanikolaou? Everything About Kylie Jenner BFF

Publish date: 2024-02-17
EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: February 17, 2020

Anastasia Karanikolaou, most famous as cosmetic mogul Kylie Jenner's best friend, is an Instagram model, influencer, and former Viner.

Anastasia started her career off as a model. She worked for Khloe Kardashian's clothing line, Good American. This made her a well-known figure in the fashion scene.

Capitalizing on her fame as a model, she has built a career in the entrepreneurial sector. She has a bathing suit collaboration called TJ Swim, which she started in November of 2019. She designs clothes with them and has 12 distinct designs of her own.

Anastasia's Personal Life: Past Boyfriends

Sammy Wilkinson was Anastasia's boyfriend. Sammy and Anastasia were friends for a long time before they started dating in 2016.

Anastasia Karanikolaou with ex-boyfriend Sammy Wilkinson (Photo: Life and Style Mag)

The couple broke up in 2017. However, the split wasn't a bad one, and they remained friends after the break-up.

The former couple even went on a vacation together in April 2019, and she was present in Sammy's sister's bachelorette party and wedding in August that same year.

Then, after a while, Anastasia was linked with another Viner, Nash Grier. However, the alleged duo never confirmed their relationship. 

In 2019, Anastasia started a relationship with Zane Hijazi. He is a Viner and a travel director for logistics companies.

However, they broke up before November of 2019.

Rumors About Dating Tyler Cameron

The Instagram personality was rumored to be going out with The Bachelorette star Tyler Cameron. The rumors about them getting together started a few months before the start of 2020.

And in January 2020, they were seen celebrating New Year's eve together along with their friends. However, representatives for Anastasia came out and denied the relationship. 

Parents And Family

Anastasia's father is Periklis Nikolaos Karanikolaou. He is a Greek-born broker who works for the World Financial Group.

Her stepmother is Nicolette Karanikolaou. Anastasia has not revealed any information about her birth mom.

The social media star is not the only child of her parents. She has an older sister named Alexia Karanikolaou. She is a hairstylist and frequently appears on her sister's social media accounts.

Bio And Interesting Facts About Anastasia Karanikolaou: Age, Height, Surgery

  • Anastasia was born on the 9th of June 1997 in Los Angeles, California, United States.
  • She stands at the height of 5 feet 7 inches or 1.70 meters. Her weight is around 60 kgs or 132 lbs.
  • Anastasia has had multiple cosmetic surgeries over the years. She has had breast implant surgery and plastic surgery; she talked about her operations on a piece with Harper's Bazaar in February 2017.
  • The model also underwent Lasik eye surgery with Kylie Jenner in October of 2019; She talked about it in a video uploaded to her YouTube channel.
  • She has a huge fan following on her social media accounts. Her Instagram, @stassiebaby, has more than 6.8 million followers. Similarly, her Twitter, @stassiebaby, has over 204.8 thousand followers.
  • Anastasia has been friends with Kylie Jenner and the Kardashians for over ten years.
