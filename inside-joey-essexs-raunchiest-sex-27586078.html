During the peak of the global pandemic, Joey's ex-girlfriend Brenda Santos confessed that the passionate pair often had sex every day.

The 22-year-old Brazilian model revealed how the reality king fell head over heels for her and her sexy “bum-bum”.

They shared a whirlwind relationship together, however the spark between them soon went out after five months together during the national lockdown.

She opened up about their romance to The Sun as she explained how they enjoyed an active sex life together.

The starlet told the publication: “We would have sex every day. Sometimes we would do it many times a day, depending on the mood.

“He was giving and generous in bed. Joey said he loved my body, especially my ‘bum-bum’ as you say in Brazil. It was intense and memorable. He’s a very passionate person like myself.

“I laughed when I saw the pictures of him playfully grabbing my bottom. He’s so cheeky. He kept saying I was beautiful all the time.

“We had a great chemistry. From the beginning there was a real-physical connection between us. I felt the energy and we just let go.”

Slept with '20 people'

Later on in the interview, the brunette beauty went on shut down rumours about the number of people Joey has slept with.

Currently, the TV star claims to have only slept with 20 people in his life despite his huge stardom up and down the country.

However, the model went on to explain how that number was more likely triple that in real life after she shed light on his playboy behaviour.

Brenda added: “I can’t believe he claims he has only slept with 20 people - I could name 20 people just off my Facebook friends alone.

“I reckon it is probably triple that, which I guess is still lower than a lot of people would imagine.

“He is always trying to protect his reputation and make himself look more clean-cut, but nope, womaniser he is!”

Bad bedroom skills

However, his wild bedroom skills weren’t loved by all of his ex-girlfriends after is former co-star Sam Faiers confessed that sex with the TV star “wasn’t amazing”.

In her autobiography titled Living Life The Essex Way, she opened up about her on-again off-again relationship with the star.

The blonde bombshell told readers how their life in the bedroom wasn’t exactly perfect.

She wrote: “As for sex, I made Joey wait about five weeks. Our sex life was just OK.

“Don’t get me wrong, it wasn’t bad – he has a great body and he was very sweet – but he just seemed quite inexperienced.

“I want more from a man in bed,” she added.

Back in 2014, the loving couple were in the throes of love as they regularly updated their loyal legion of fans about their romance online.

And the couple took their honesty to a whole new level when the hunk shared a steamy post-sex snap on his Instagram account.

In full view of his loyal legion of fans, the TV star shared a seemingly naked snap of the duo in bed with Sam nuzzling Joey’s neck as he reached out to take a picture of the intimate moment.

He captioned the saucy snap: “One night means everything.”
