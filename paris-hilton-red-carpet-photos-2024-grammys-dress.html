Paris Hilton Sparkles in Sequin Aquamarine Gown at 2024 Grammys

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Paris Hilton arrived at the 2024 Grammys looking like the most glamorous mermaid.

The hotel heiress, DJ, and entrepreneur walked the red carpet at Los Angeles’s Crypto.com Arena in a shimmery sequin aquamarine gown and matching glittery accessories, paying homage to the ongoing mermaidcore trend.

The “Stars Are Blind” singer’s dress, from Reem Acra’s Spring/Summer 2024 ready-to-wear collection, featured a corsetlike bodice in nude mesh, with strands of sequins in a soft blue-green hue cascading down in scooped layers. The piece also had thin straps and side cutouts broken up by a sequin-embellished waist strap.

courtesy of Paris Hilton via Instagram

To add to the glitz, Hilton wore white diamond drop earrings and a large diamond cuff by Bucherer Fine Jewellery. She also held a glittery light blue clutch with Hilton emblazoned in gold in the front.

Hilton’s sparkly blue eye shadow perfectly matched her gown, as did her chic French manicure (French manis are back, by the way). To finish the look, she wore her hair in a sleek, straight ’do.

Lester Cohen//Getty Images

Husband Carter Reum walked the carpet with her, looking dapper in a sleek navy suit.

Lester Cohen//Getty Images

Ever the maximalist, Hilton wore another sparkly gown last night, at the pre-Grammys gala at the Beverly Hilton hotel. The piece was a slinky golden slipdress with sequins all over and a subtle leg slit. Hilton carried a gold leather mini clutch and accessorized with layers of diamonds, including dramatic drop earrings with white and yellow diamonds, a matching bracelet, and her massive diamond engagement ring from Reum. Her makeup look was equally glossy, and she wore her hair in a silky, side-parted, wavy style.
