Florida Georgia Line Throw Back With 'Dig Your Roots' Cover

Publish date: 2024-07-08

Florida Georgia Line are showing fans what it really means to be proud of where you come from with their Dig Your Roots album cover, which features real-life photos of the guys when they were just little tykes. An adorable and tiny Brian Kelley peeks out from under a cowboy hat, which accessorizes only a diaper and cowboy boots, while Tyler Hubbard prepares for future off-roading on a tiny four-wheeler.

“Our album cover says it all,” shares Kelley in a press release. "Tyler and I are continuing to grow musically and personally. We really wanted this album to reflect who we are and our lives in this moment, but also where we came from.”

The band’s third studio album is slated for release August 26, and FGL couldn’t be more excited to share who they are with their fans, starting with the cover art.

“We both started out playing guitar, singing in church and writing songs so we wanted to let fans see our foundation — a deeper side to us. We couldn’t have picked a more fitting title or image for Dig Your Roots,” Hubbard adds.

With the new album comes five exclusive bundles for fans to choose from in advance of its release, from now until Aug. 14, starting at $25, each named after tracks on the record. More than half of the songs on the album are written by Kelley and Hubbard, and their latest single, “H.O.L.Y.”, written by busbee, Nate Cyphert and William Larsen, has already earned the top spot on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart six weeks running.
