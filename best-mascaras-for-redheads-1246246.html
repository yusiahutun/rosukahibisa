    How to Select the Best Mascara for Redheads?

    Color: The first thing that comes to mind when choosing the right match for your pale or gingerly lashes is the color. A prominent black stands out, that’s why you need to opt for shades of brown, red, and burgundy. Go for a color that best matches your natural tone rather than a deeply pigmented color. A lighter shade will enhance your natural appearance. 

    Staying power: How well your mascara stays put on your lashes is an essential aspect of weighing your options. A smudge-proof, water-proof, sweat, and tear-proof mascara will ensure that your lashes look fresh, long, and voluminous throughout the day. 

    Formula: Lastly, look for the ingredients that encompass the mascara recipe. If you have sensitive skin, make sure the formula is hypoallergenic and approved by dermatologists. Your mascara should also be easily removed with warm water or micellar water for a hassle-free experience. 

    Wand: The bristles are made of plastic, silicones, or nylon along with varying shapes and curves. Look for a nice wand that separates the lashes and applies mascara without clumping or irritating the eyes. 

    How to Apply Mascara Like a Pro?

    Applying mascara doesn’t seem like rocket science, however, the right steps can help you achieve fuller and long-lasting lashes. 

    Step 1: Use a lash curler to firmly curl and lift your lashes. 

    Step 2: Apply a lash primer for better-enhanced results and create a base for the mascara.

    Step 3: once the primer has dried, begin coating your mascara with the wand. Ensure that your wand is tightly close to the roots and gently wiggle the brush upwards. 

    Step 4: Apply as many strokes as required for your desired finish.

    FAQ's

    Is there a specific mascara for redheads?

    To match the redheads’ lashes, there are shades of brown and red mascaras that naturally enhance the look.

    What mascara color works best for redheads?

    Shades of burgundy, red, ginger, cocoa, brown, auburn, and chestnut go best with redheads’ lashes.

    Should redheads wear mascara?

    Yes, mascaras are for everyone. However, for a perfectly natural appearance matching the right shade is important and a brown and red tinted mascara is ideal for redheads.

    Are waterproof mascaras a good choice for redheads?

    Yes, waterproof mascaras are good for redheads and anyone willing to achieve a long-stay mascara look.
