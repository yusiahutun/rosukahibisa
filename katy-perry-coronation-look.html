Katy Perry's Plunging Gold Gown Won King Charles' Coronation

Publish date: 2024-06-23

King Charles III’s coronation proved to be a historic affair. Not only was the official Saturday ceremony memorable (Louis stole the show), but on Sunday, the celebratory concert included performances by Katy Perry, Lionel Richie, British pop group Take That, opera stars Andrea Bocelli, Bryn Terfel, Freya Ridings and Alexis Ffrench. For the official coronation ceremony on Saturday, Perry brought her American pop star diva energy to Westminster Abbey, arriving in a light purple two-piece dress with a matching hat and gloves.

She kept it prim and proper when she arrived with Edward Enninful, editor-in-chief of British Vogue. Perry’s lavender ensemble was fitting for the coronation day, since the color purple in the Bible is associated with royalty. That nod to the momentous day came through from top to bottom in Perry’s look, starting with her off-center hat and all the way down to the sheath dress covered by a short sleeve jacket and pinned with a flower. She accented the look with a three-strand pearl choker (complete with a diamond encrusted Sovereign’s Orb—another nod to the coronation), a white hand bag and white heels.

While her coronation ‘fit was a lavender lewk to love, the countdown was on for her to show up and show out at Sunday’s performance.

At the performance held at Windsor Castle, Perry arrived in an over-the-top gold-metallic Vivienne Westwood gown with matching long opera gloves. According to People, Perry performed “Roar” and “Firework,” fitting for the coronation of a new monarch.

You might be wondering why Perry was chosen to perform at King Charles’ coronation. According to Forbes, the King and Perry met during an event in Mumbai in 2019. Charles appointed Perry as an ambassador for the British Asian Trust just a year later. Charles said Perry has a “long-standing commitment to charitable causes around the world.” Lionel Richie is also connected to Charles. According to Forbes, he was appointed by Charles as the first global ambassador for The Prince’s Trust.

Since both Perry and Richie were in England for the coronation, Ed Sheeran and Alanis Morissette temporarily replaced them on American Idol, per page Page Six. For now, all eyes are on the Brits.
