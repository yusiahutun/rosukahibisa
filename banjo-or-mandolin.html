Banjo or Mandolin? Chris Coyier

Publish date: 2024-09-17

I’ve been thinking a lot about adding more personal stuff to my blog, and this video just completely sold me! Thanks!

“Chris Coyer” used to be synonymous with “CSS” in my head, but suddenly you’ve transformed from a blog into a breathing person with family and musical talents :)

Those unfamiliar with contemporary bluegrass should check out Punch Brothers / Chris Thile / Nickel Creek. I think Phosphorescent Blues and the Nickel Creek self-titled are two good starter albums.

IMO mandolin is more fun for solo play, while banjo is more fun in a band.
