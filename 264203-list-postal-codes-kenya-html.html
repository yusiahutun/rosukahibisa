Publish date: 2024-09-13

Searching for a postal code of a certain Kenyan city? No problem, in the following article you will find a full list of postal codes in major Kenyan cities and towns. Ichichi postal code, Gatunga postal code, postal codes Nairobi and much more.

Postal codes in Kenya

Postal code, also known in English speaking countries as pin code, zip code, air-code etc is a combination of numbers and letters, in some situations include spaces or special symbols. This is made, to help the post workers to easily sort mail and bring it to the correct destination.

In most cases, the postal codes in Kenya are sorted by geographical areas. However, there are special postal codes Kenya assigned to individual addresses or to institutions. This is made, because such institutions as governmental departments receive a lot of mail almost every day.
