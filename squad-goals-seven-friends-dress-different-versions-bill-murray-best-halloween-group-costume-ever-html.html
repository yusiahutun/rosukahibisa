Friends dress as different versions of Bill Murray in the best Halloween group costume

Publish date: 2024-06-01

One group of friends paid tribute to Bill Murray this Halloween, racking up prizes for their impressively coordinated group costumes for the fifth year in a row.

Each year, Auburn Salcedo and her six friends from Nebraska choose to dress up as one beloved actor in seven iconic roles, with previous choices including Robin Williams and Will Ferrell.

This year's knockout costumes featured Bill Murray in the iconic films Garfield, Space Jam, Caddyshack, The Life Aquatic, Ghostbusters, Kingpin and What About Bob?

One group of friends paid tribute to Bill Murray in seven iconic roles (pictured left to right: Bill Murray in Garfield, Space Jam, Caddyshack, The Life Aquatic, Ghostbusters, Kingpin and What About Bob?)

Fat cat: One woman dressed up as Garfield, which Murray said he signed on to because he thought it was written by Joel Coen of the Coen brothers. The script was penned by Joel Cohen

I believe I can fly: Another woman donned a Tune Squad jersey as Bill Murray, who played himself in the 1996 classic Space Jam

Au revoir, gopher: One woman donned a camouflage bucket hat and got hold of a stuffed gopher for her portrayal of Murray in the cult film Caddyshack

The seven women first bonded as coworkers toiling under 'a boss from hell' and remained best friends after they all quit. 

The first took on Johnny Depp in 2012, before Jim Carrey, Will Ferrell and Robin Williams got the copycat treatment in subsequent years.

Salcedo said they ride on the excitement of their costumes and pick a new actor within 24 hours of Halloween so they have a full year to prepare.

'Once the actor is decided, we all basically quickly name who our top pick is. Within minutes...it's done!' she said, adding, 'We found that it is better to just plan the next actor and make our picks when we are all still hyped about it.' 

And their attention to detail was certainly on display this year.  

The group highlighted key roles from Murray's colorful career, from his voice acting work as the title character in the animated film Garfield, to his swashbuckling portrayal of a 'parascyhology' professor in the 1984 classic Ghostbusters.

One friend who pulled off Murray as Ernie McCracken in Kingpin managed to make a replica of his trademark bowling ball, while the Ghostbusters fan created an impressive show of props, including a ghost trap and a proton pack.  

Leopard fish? One friend took on Murray in The Life Acquatic with Steve Zissou, dressing up as the legendary oceanographer on the hunt for a jaguar shark that ate his best friend

Who you gonna call? A friend showed off an impressive display of props in her Ghostbusters costume that included a proton pack as well as a ghost trap

I didn't want to get beat by a guy with a hook: Salcedo's Ernie McCraken costume was spot on, with the comb over and custom bowling ball to match

I'm sailing! One friend dressed as Murray in the black comedy What About Bob? and wore t-shirt that read: 'Don't hassle me I'm local' and carried around the Baby Steps book

Salcedo, who posts a photo of the costumes on Reddit every year previously said: 'We are totally thrilled people enjoy this as much as we do!' 

Anyone hoping to challenge her friends for the local Best Group Costume award better step up their game, since Salcedo said they hoped to continue the tradition into old age.

She said: 'We all plan to live in the same assisted living complex where we will dress up each year until we slowly die off one by one.' 

Last year, the women went as different variations of Robin Williams in the films Mrs. Doubtfire, Mork, Jumanji, Popeye, Hook, Aladdin, and The Birdcage.

The friends also make sure to stand in the same order when they pose for pictures every Halloween, so it's easy to pick out how they each transformed from year to year.
