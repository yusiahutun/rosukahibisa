They say Aaron B. is such a great catch. His smile lights up every room he walks into, and he wears his heart on his sleeve. Now, with these very traits and more, he decided to go on The Bachelorette Season 20 to fall in love again.

Aaron is one among the 25 suitors vying for the season’s bachelorette, Charity Lawson’s heart. So, in this writing let’s get to know him better, in and out of the dating game competition.

Aaron Bryant On The Bachelorette

According to Reality Steve’s announcement on 11 April 2023, Aaron Bryant, AKA Aaron B., also is one of Charity Lawson’s final four men. (The others were revealed as Xavier Bonner, Joey Graziadei, and Dotun Olubeko.)

What’s more, Steve also maintained when filming for each of these contestants’ hometown episodes takes place. For Aaron, he said it was on April 15th.

Aaron’s BIO for The Bachelorette describes him as a passionate, understanding, patient, and self-proclaimed hopeless romantic. It also goes on to cite that Aaron is “truly” ready to find his perfect match and hopes his future wife is outgoing, emotionally intelligent, and thoughtful.

Aaron also told people on The Bachelorette that his last long-term relationship did not work out, and he is therefore excited for the chance to fall in love again. He was clearly really excited for the Bachelorette journey to begin.

So you know, Aaron also was previously also seen in an episode of ABC’s The Final Straw.

Aaron Bryant Job

Aaron Bryant has been hustling at his job in software sales for a while now. Per LinkedIn, Aaron in his last job worked full-time as a major market sales executive at Paycor in San Diego, California from June 2022 until March 2023.

In May 2023, Aaron joined 1st Class Real Estate SD. With pride, the company welcomed Aaron as their brand’s new agent coming.

It seems Aaron also has some great passions as a realtor. He comes with years of enterprise sales and is laser-focused on helping those looking to achieve their home-buying, selling, and wealth-building dreams. He’s already meeting with buyers and is passionate about providing generous communication, dedication, and transparency in order to help his clients achieve their goals.

For a year before, he was a uniform sales representative at Cintas in San Diego, California. Even prior, he was a senior associate at United Rentals.

Aaron also comes with a Bachelor of Science in Business Management and Marketing Double from Tulane University – A.B. Freeman School of Business (2011 – 2015).

When he is not doing his job, he loves listening to Tyler, the Creator’s music, reading James Patterson novels, and enjoying some delicious Cajun food.

Aaron Bryant Family

Aaron’s parents are Edward N Bryant and Ethel Louise Bryant and his siblings are Edward, Jr. & Erick.

On 14 March 2017, Aaron took to his social media to adorably write “Happy 26th anniversary to my amazing parents ❤️ you two have set a great example” next to their parents’ picture.

Aaron has always felt very fortunate to have his family supporting his path and his purpose. One can often hear him say “My dad would always say…” kind of statements.

His father Edward N Bryant turned 59 years old in June 2023 and his mother Ethel reached the age of 53 in December a year before.

Aaron’s brother Erick (on IG @e_boiiii9) is seemingly a freelance worker. He turned 33 in April 2023. The other brother, Edward, turned 31 in August 2022.

Per LinkedIn, he is a USAF Veteran, UTSA graduate in Business Admin/Mgmt., and computer user support specialist. He served in the United States Air Force from October 2009 through October 2017.

Aaron’s paternal grandparents were Thomas C. Bryant, Sr., and Erma J. Bryant. They were married for 62 years before they both passed away one after another. Both were also very devoted and dedicated Christians, among other things.
