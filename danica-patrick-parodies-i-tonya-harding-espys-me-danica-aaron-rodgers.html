Danica Patrick Parodies 'I, Tonya' at ESPYS with 'Me, Danica'

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Danica Patrick didn't get rave reviews for hosting the ESPYS on Wednesday night, but she did crush it in a pre-taped "I, Tonya" parody, "Me, Danica."

The IndyCar driver and GoDaddy spokeswoman took two characters for a spin in the faux trailer, portraying both a trashier version of herself -- basically Tonya Harding if she raced cars instead of ice skating -- and her alcoholic, emotionally abusive mother who looks and acts a lot like Allison Janney from "I, Tonya."

Danica learned to drive when she was nine, because that's when I got my fifth DUI," the mother character tells the camera. "With her it was always GoDaddy this and GoDaddy that. What about GoMommy?"
