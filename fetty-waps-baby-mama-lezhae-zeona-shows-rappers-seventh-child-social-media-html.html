Fetty Wap's baby mama Lezhae Zeona shows off rapper's seventh child

Publish date: 2024-04-01

Fetty Wap's baby mama Lezhae Zeona was seen in new shots on Instagram posing with her two kids with the rap artist.

In a series of shots posted on the Instagram page of Hair So Fab, Zeona was seen posing with three-year-old daughter Zaviera Maxwell and two-month-old son, Zy.
