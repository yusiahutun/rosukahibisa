He told AVN: “I’ve grown a lot since I started working in the adult industry and I want my name to reflect that I take my job seriously.”

Small Hands, whose real name is Aaron Thompson, is one of the most popular male porn stars.

He is married to tattooed porn star Joanna Angel and she is credited for the inspiration for his unique name.

Thompson was about to star in his first adult film and was asked to sign documents which included his porn star name when he suddenly thought of the pseudonym Small Hands.

He said: "Joanna has always made fun of me because I have little baby hands for a grown man, and literally I just wrote it down because I couldn’t think of anything else."

To him, the pseudonym was both stupid and hilarious and he figured it wouldn’t get much use after what he assumed would be a one-time porn shoot for his girlfriend’s production company, Burning Angel.

But one thing led to another, and pretty soon Thompson was booking scenes with other big-time performers for a handful of different studios, eventually becoming one of the top male talents in the industry.

Ben Dover, real name Simon Honey, is the star of more 150 porn films and has directed a similar amount.

Seymore Butts is an actor and director who specialises in the gonzo genre of pornography.
