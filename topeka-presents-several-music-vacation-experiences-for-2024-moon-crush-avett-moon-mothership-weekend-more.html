Topeka Presents Several Music Vacation Experiences For 2024 Moon Crush-Avett Moon, Mothership Weeken

Publish date: 2024-06-07

MIRAMAR BEACH, FL (CelebrityAccess) – Topeka, known for creating the innovative and much-lauded, no-line boutique Music Vacation experience, confirms a brand-new weekend taking place on the shores of Miramar Beach, Florida, this Fall—The Avett Brothers’ “Moon Crush – Avett Moon” is set for November 8-10, 2024.

After first performing at Moon Crush 2023, The Avett Brothers return to host their own iteration of a music vacation weekend, featuring two headline sets plus performances by Greensky Bluegrass, Band of Horses, Shakey Graves, Grace Potter, and more!

The weekend joins Topeka’s ever-growing lineup of curated experiences in 2024, including Brandi Carlile’s second annual “Mothership Weekend,” celebrating Mother’s Day, May 10-12, with Bonnie Raitt, Sara Bareilles, Black Pumas, Nickel Creek, The Hanseroth Twins, Devon Gilfillian and S.G. Goodman.

Tedeschi Trucks Band’s “Sun, Sand and Soul Beach Weekend” will see the band take the stage for two of the three nights, plus performances by JJ Grey & Mofro, The Teskey Brothers, Blackberry Smoke, St. Paul & The Broken Bones, Maggie Rose, Duane Betts, and Karina Rykman – set for May 2-4, 2024

Moon Crush will return for its fourth year with a “Pink Moon” installment April 18-21, 2024, boasting a stellar lineup of Noah Kahan, Lake Street Dive, Marcus King, The Revivalists, Charley Crockett, Trombone Shorty & Orleans Avenue, The Head and the Heart, Dawes, Larkin Poe and more.

The following weekend will see Lauren Daigle’s “Kaleidoscope Beach Weekend,” April 25-27, 2024. There will be three evenings of music with performances by Daigle all three nights, including a special worship night, plus headliners Jon Batiste, Natalie Hemby, Kari Jobe, Ellie Holcomb, and more.

Topeka unveiled their one-of-a-kind and original music vacations in 2021 with the first installment of Moon Crush—the first multi-day destination event in the country following COVID-19. They immediately gained nationwide recognition for their innovative take on the classic music festival.

Attendees at these Topeka-organized events are in for a treat. Days are filled with the beauty of nearby beaches and various vacation activities, setting the stage for unforgettable evenings of music from renowned artists gracing a substantial outdoor stage. To enhance the experience, family-friendly accommodations are readily available, ensuring a welcoming atmosphere for all.

For those seeking a more intimate and personalized experience, the option to reserve a private Cove for friends and family is a game-changer. This exclusive space offers a front-row seat to the performances. It includes the added perks of a “no-line” policy and on-demand food and drink services provided by the efficient Topeka team, all within an impressive five-minute turnaround. Introducing these Coves eliminates the hassle of waiting in long concession lines, allowing attendees to invest more time in crafting lasting memories, whether it’s “Crushin'” on loved ones or enjoying the musical brilliance of favorite artists. It’s a thoughtful and convenient touch that adds an extra layer of enjoyment to the entire Music Vacation experience.
