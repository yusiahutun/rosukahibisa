Best novels to read for students: 10 must-read books in English

Publish date: 2024-03-30

Choosing the best novels to read for students can be a challenging task. One must determine many factors to ensure they have selected the right book. For example, does the novel align with the interests of the student? Choosing a novel should also be based on a student’s reading level. A student’s reading level helps determine their comprehension and fluency of the language.

The best novels to read for students are those that provide opportunities for a student to expand their vocabulary. Most importantly, the best novels must offer valuable life lessons, stimulate the brain, and have engaging stories that make learning more enjoyable.

10 best novels to read for students

Which novel is best for students to read? The best books for students' success cover many genres, from classic literature to contemporary fiction. These novels offer valuable life lessons and engaging stories, making it easier for students to learn English or improve their language skills. Here are 10 best novels to read according to Amber Student:

1. Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels chronicling the lives of a young wizard called Harry Potter and his fellow student friends, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley. The novel has wide acceptance worldwide because it has a captivating story filled with magic and adventure.

The novel series is the best for students because it engages and sparks their imaginations. The book can foster a love of reading by sparking a reader's imagination, which is crucial in helping a student learn English.

2. To Kill A Mocking Bird

To Kill A Mocking Bird is one of the best novels for students to read because of its captivating and engaging storyline. The book is more of a drama and coming-of-age story that deals with many societal themes, including morality, racism, and injustice.

The novel has a clear and straightforward language that is easy for any student to understand, especially those learning English at a beginner level. Furthermore, its engaging story makes students want to read more, making learning about sentence structures in English easier.

3. Fantastic Mr Fox

Fantastic Mr Fox is a children’s novel and one of the best short novels in English for students. The novel is about a character named Fox, his wife, and four children that outsmart local farmers to raise poultry and feed themselves and their friends.

Fantastic Mr Fox is a good book for children because of its unique story. Also, it can significantly improve a child’s language skills because the story is engaging, funny, and memorable. Students can benefit immensely from the novel’s interesting adjectives and grammar structures.

4. The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time

The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Night-Time follows the story of a 15-year-old protagonist with Asperger’s Syndrome. The boy is an excellent mathematician. However, he has behavioural difficulties that make him an outsider.

The novel has been in educational settings worldwide because of the lengths the main character goes to investigate the death of his neighbour’s dog. However, it can teach English students more about creating compelling narratives.

5. The Power of Positive Thinking

The Power of Positive Thinking is one of the best motivational novels for students in English. It is written by Norman Vincent Peale, who offers practical exercises on reframing negative thoughts, building self-confidence, and attracting positive outcomes.

The Power of Positive Thinking is more of an empowering book. However, it has a rich vocabulary that can help students understand specific English words. Also, the novel can improve a person’s comprehension skills and develop better sentence patterns.

6. Do Hard Things: A Teenage Rebellion Against Low Expectations

Do Hard Things: A Teenage Rebellion Against Low Expectations is a book about a growing movement of young people who rebel against today’s culture’s low expectations. They decide to do hard things to meet the challenges they face.

The book’s author uses clear and to-the-point language, making it easier for readers to understand. Although the book targets teenagers, adults can also read it to see sentence structure, grammar, and the rich use of vocabulary.

7. The Handmaid's Tale

The Handmaid’s Tale by Margaret Atwood is a dystopian novel exploring religious politics, power, and gender. The book has rich English text that makes it perfect for individuals that want to learn English at an advanced level.

The book uses complex language and literary techniques that can be challenging for beginners. Furthermore, its sentence structure and vocabulary are more advanced for those developing their English skills.

The book can be a goldmine for English literature writers or poets who want to understand how to initially present large quantities of information and slowly enlighten the reader as the story progresses.

8. Twelfth Night

Twelfth Night is a comedic play by William Shakespeare. And like most books by Shakespeare, you always expect the book to have rich English text, Twelfth Night is no exception.

Twelfth Night has elaborate language and wordplay. It is also filled with intricate puns and metaphors that need a broader understanding of the English language. Thus, the book can be a great addition to learners that want to understand English at an advanced level.

9. Great Expectations

Great Expectations is a novel by Charles Dickens. It is one of the best works of literature for English students. Dickens uses complex language to write the book. Thus, it is the perfect book for students who want to fully grasp English literature.

The language has a rich vocabulary, intricate sentence structure, and many instances of figurative language. Furthermore, Dickens employs wordplay, allusions, and metaphors to make the book more engaging. But the book requires patience to read through it because it has over 500 pages.

10. And Still I Rise: A Book of Poems

And Still I Rise: A Book of Poems is one of the best novels to read to improve English. The book contains poems written by Maya Angelou. The poems explore many themes, including the experiences of African-Americans, resilience, and empowerment.

The book is perfect for people that want to learn how to write English poems. Maya Angelou showcases her lyrical and evocative writing style in all her poems. She also adds imagery, rhythm and language to portray specific societal scenarios.
