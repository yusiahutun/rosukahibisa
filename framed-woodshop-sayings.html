There's no doubt that there will be some hardship and frustration when working the woodshop. Whether it's a piece of cypress that won't cooperate or the wrong cut because of inaccurate measurements, there's always a bright side and a lesson to be learned. Ben's favorite sayings have comforted many a woodworker over the years. If this sounds like you, it might be time to hang these signs in your wood shop, too.
