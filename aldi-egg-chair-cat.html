Aldi launches an egg chair just for cats

Publish date: 2024-05-13

Recently, in a move of incredible ingenuity, Aldi released an egg chair for cats that's just £29.99. In the past year, 3.2 million households have welcomed a new pet into their home, so we’re expecting similar high demand for this new pet edition of the egg chair.

Meanwhile, Aldi shows no sign of slowing down! The brand is soon to restock its own two-seater hanging egg chair to join its growing family of cocoon chairs.

Read more: The Aldi Egg Chair: Will it be restocked?

What is the Aldi Cat Egg Chair?

The adorable mini egg chair was launched as part of Aldi's Eco Pet range and is only available online.

Every product in the range is sustainable; the cat egg chair is made with hand-woven natural wicker. The egg itself hangs from a strong but minimalist white metal frame to keep your pet safe. It also comes with a removable inner cushion so your cat is cosy and comfortable whenever they fancy a snooze.

Are there any Aldi Cat Egg Chair alternatives?

We have a feeling that the cat egg chair is going to sell out just as quickly as its human counterpart. So if you don’t manage to snag one when it’s in stock and simply can’t wait to treat your pet, check out our favourite cat chairs below. All of these rival the Aldi design and are an equally purr-fect alternative.

Archie & Oscar Cat Perch

This chair is pretty similar to Aldi’s without the hanging design. It has a robust metal frame, cosy cushion and contemporary, hand-woven rattan design that keeps your cat feeling chic even as it sleeps.

Cat claws are no match for the durable metal frame while the raised design allows for air circulation to keep your feline comfortable. It can carry up to 10kg.

£69.99 at Wayfair

Amazon Macrame Cat Hammock

Feel like your cat would be more into a hammock rather than an egg chair? This cat hammock has been handcrafted using 100% cotton rope. The natural colour works well in any room as does the elegant design.

It's a sturdy basket so even if your cat turns up its nose at the hammock, you could use it as a decorative plant holder instead.

£17.99 at Amazon

PawHut Wicker Cat Bed Rattan Den

We’re completely smitten with this cat house! The cat ear design is unique yet minimalist. The house is made from rattan, metal wire and comes with a cosy cushion for extra comfort.

£43.99 at Amazon

PawHut Raised Kitten Bed

Cosy and tactile, the PawHut Raised Kitten Bed is a practical, yet aesthetic choice. Its warm neutrals complement minimalist decor but your cat will love its soft, cushioned interior. Its exterior doubles as a scratcher and as it's elevated off the ground, during the warmer months, your feline friend won't get too hot.

£37.99 at ManoMano

Cox and Cox Cat Cocoon

If you have your heart set on the egg design, this cat cocoon is another perfect option. It’s made from natural banana leaf so each chair will be a unique blend of natural hues.

£44.99 at The Range
