Ark: Survival Ascended: ALL New Dinos You Can Find

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Are you looking for a full list of all new dinos in Ark Survival Ascended? Here’s every new dinosaur in the game.

While it’s a new game, Ark Survival Ascended only has five new dinosaurs that weren’t available in Evolved.

In this guide, I will list these five new dinos and showcase where to find them in case you want to tame them.

If you’re just starting on a server and want to progress fast, don’t forget to also check out our guide on how to level up quickly in Ark Survival Ascended.

All New Dinosaurs in Ark Survival Ascended

While it’s a new game, Ark Survival Ascended doesn’t have many new dinos. Specifically, there are six entries on this list and five of them haven’t been released yet and are only planned for future DLC releases.

Nevertheless, it’s worth getting excited about them, since we know a little information about their behavior and appearance.

Without further delay, here are all the new dinos in Ark Survival Ascended and what to expect from them.

Dreadnoughtus

The Dreadnoughtus will be introduced when the Extinction DLC arrives in ASA. It’s a Tameable and Rideable herbivore dinosaur but not much more is known about it yet.

Fasolasuchus

The Fasolasuchus is planned to be released with the Scorched Earth map and DLC in ASA. It is a dino from the Late Triassic time that’s a Carnivore and has an overall aggressive behavior.

Gigantoraptor

The Gigantoraptor is going to be introduced with the Ragnarok DLC in Ark Survival Ascended. It’s an Omnivore dinosaur that can’t be tamed and has Territorial behavior.

Shastasaurus

Shastasaurus will be introduced with the Center DLC and it’s a tameable dino with a Carnivore diet and a Curious behavior.

Yi Ling

The Yi Ling is another DLC dinosaur that will be added with the Aberration DLC. It’s a tameable dinosaur that’s an Omnivore and has Aloof behavior.

Rhyniognatha

The last new addition is also the best dino to tame in Ark Survival Ascended and that’s the Rhyniognatha.

It can fly, swim, pick up items and dinosaurs, and attack enemies from afar.

You can find it all over the Red Woods. To tame a Rhyniognatha in Ark, you’ll need to give Rhyniognatha Pheromone (gained by killing a Rhyniognatha) to one of your large dinosaurs and bring a low HP female Rhyniognattha near it to impregnate it.

Wrapping up

In conclusion, these are all the new dinos you can find in Ark Survival Ascended. While most of them aren’t available in the game and will only be released with future DLCs, it’s nice to know what to expect from them.
