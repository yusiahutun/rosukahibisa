Joe Jonas, Camilla Belle Call It Quits

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Teen girls, rejoice: Joe Jonas is a single man. The 19-year-old singer and actress Camilla Belle, 22, have called it quits.

"Yes, it's true," Belle's rep tells Usmagazine.com. "[Joe and Camilla] have broken up. There is no third party involved and they care deeply about each other and will remain friends."
