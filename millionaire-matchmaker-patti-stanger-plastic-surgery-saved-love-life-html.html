The break-over: Millionaire Matchmaker Patti Stanger admits to plastic surgery

Millionaire Matchmaker Patti Stanger has admitted that going under the knife helped her get ahead in the dating game.

After her seven-year relationship with real-estate executive Andy Friedman ended in 2010, Ms Stanger said plastic surgery boosted her confidence.

'I had my first breast reduction and lift five years ago,' Ms Stanger, 52, told Life & Style.

'Then I had another one. I felt skinny and young again!'

She explained to host Andy Cohen on Watch What Happens Live that in 2011, she also went to her doctor for a facelift.

'He wouldn’t give it to me,' she said. Instead, she says that he suggested an eye lift to correct a procedure called ptosis, or droopy lids. 

Afterwards, she told the magazine: 'I felt pretty and like I could go out, date and compete with all the young girls.'

Shortly afterward, the Bravo personality met mortgage broker David Krause, 42, on an online dating site.

'When I met David [Krause], he thought I was younger than I was! He didn’t care, though, when he found out my real age.'

She introduced him to her friends at her 51st birthday bash at Koi restaurant, and the couple has now been dating for more than a year.

On an episode of Millionaire Matchmaker, Mr Krause presented her with a diamond-encrusted David Yurman ring.

But Ms Stanger denies reports that the two are engaged, explaining that gift is a promise ring.

Ms Stanger advises her clients on the show that a proposal should come after a year of dating, and does not recommend moving in before marriage.

But for Mr Krause, she seems to be willing to break her own rules: The couple recently moved in together.

Unlike many TV personalities, Ms Stanger has been outspoken about altering her appearance.

In the past she has admitted to getting Botox injections, though she says that they help her cluster headaches.

Changing her look: Ms Stanger, pictured in March 2010 (left) and post-procedure in February 2012 (right), has admitted that plastic surgery boosted her confidence

She also lost 20 pounds, and showed off her slimmer figure by walking the runway for Guria swimwear in a bikini and sheer Kaftan.

'We’re all getting plastic surgery,' she told Mr Cohen. 'Come on, this is the game here, and HDTV exaggerates all the features. Yeah, I’m proud of it, because we’re all doing it. Nobody’s talking about it.'
