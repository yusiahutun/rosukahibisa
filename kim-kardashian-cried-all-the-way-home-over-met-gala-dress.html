Kim Kardashian &quot;cried&quot; over 2013 Met Gala floral Givenchy dress

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Kim Kardashian’s 2013 Met Gala dress remains famous for igniting social media.

Now, the 39-year-old admits she “cried the whole way home” over the response it got.

For the gala – Kardashian’s first – she wore a floral high-neck, long-sleeved gown with a thigh-high split by Riccardo Tisci for Givenchy. At the time, the now mum-of-four was heavily pregnant with her first child, North.

Jamie McCarthy//Getty Images

Kim Kardashian with husband Kanye West at the 2013 Met Gala.

Soon after, memes about the outfit began circulating online, with a famous one comparing the look to that of Robin Williams’ character in Mrs Doubtfire (1993).

Williams himself shared the meme, captioning it “I think I wore it better!”

Larry Busacca//Getty Images

Speaking to US Vogue in a video looking back on her most notable looks, Kardashian said: “I was very pregnant, very puffy, and bloated and I was like, 'Oh god, of course the first time I go I'm gonna be huge'.”

The businesswoman explained that she never thought she’d be able to attend such an exclusive event, so was overjoyed when she got to go to the gala – themed Punk: Chaos to Couture that year – as Kanye’s plus one.

However, the night ended in tears as soon as she cottoned on to the reaction.

“I was crying, like, the whole way home because I just couldn’t believe it. There were all these memes about me and this couch,” she recalled.

Larry Busacca//Getty Images

Kim Kardashian’s Givenchy dress was the subject of many memes.

“I think Robin Williams even tweeted it, said I looked like Mrs Doubtfire. It was like this whole thing, so I just cried.”

While the controversial look wasn’t a win with many fans, Kardashian said it got approval from fashionable sources.

“I just remember the Olsen twins came up to me and loved my outfit and my gloves so none of the critics mattered because the Olsen twins approved.”

As for now, Kardashian feels good about the dress. “Now I love it, now it's sick,” she said.
