Crochet bags: the 10 best styles to shop right now

Publish date: 2024-06-21

Despite crochet's humble origins, the needlecraft technique has taken the fashion industry by storm, and is showing zero signs of stopping in 2023. It experienced something of a revival in the Swinging Sixties, transforming from solely a homemaker's art into a key fashion trend. A new generation cottoned onto the playful effect of interlocked loops, championing the vivid granny square well into the next decade.

Once deeply ingrained in the fabric of Coachella's dress code, crochet has taken a turn, having been adopted by lovers of Y2K as well as devotees of Daisy Jones & The Six.

Judging by crochet's prevalence in this season's collections, from boho bodysuits at Isabel Marant to pearl-adorned headgear Regina Pyo, the trend is here to stay. 

It has been translated into a variety of stylish forms, including slip dresses and bucket hats, and as it goes, we're thrilled to report that crochet bags are officially not a step too far, but an integral part of your summer accessory collection.

How we chose the pieces:

We've scoured the internet's top fashion sites, taking into account price, quality and style, for the crochet bags that will give your accessory game a serious upgrade. While crochet lends itself to a free-spirited, summery feel, we've chosen pieces that will elevate an outfit both this season and beyond. This season we're lusting after Polish label Magda Butrym's rosette tote as well as Mango's electric blue shoulder bag.

Hello! Fashion shares the best crochet bags to shop right now:

  • Bayia Bucket Bag

    Isabel Marant

    Crafted from canvas with a neat crochet overlay, this Isabel Marant bucket bag feels elegant and unfussy. The tan leather trim adds a beautiful, summer warmth, but it would no doubt work well with a navy trench and slouchy charcoal trousers come autumn.

  • Devana Crochet-knit Shoulder Bag

    Magda Butrym

    This elegant shoulder bag by Polish label Magda Butrym wouldn't look out of place in Sofia Richie's accessory arsenal. The voluminous satin bloom feels bang on trend for SS23 and it even has an inner drawstring pouch to keep your essentials secure.

  • T Timeless Leather-trimmed Crocheted Shoulder Bag

    Tod's

    This smart raffia shoulder bag by Tod's feels utterly ageless. The neutral colour palette feels earthy and warm, and the glint of gold hardware is a nice touch, a subtle tribute to the label's rich heritage. 

  • Crochet Tote

    Pull & Bear

    This playful tote by Pull & Bear won't break the bank, and would make for a seriously fun beach bag. The vibrant hues feel utterly retro - team with a Farrah Fawcett blow dry and platform clogs for major 1970s vibes.

  • Macram√© Mini Bag

    This rusty orange tote by Zara is giving off major Daisy Jones & The Six vibes. The chunky design adds interest, and for maximum 1970s appeal, team with bellbottom jeans, platforms and a care-free attitude.

  • Floral Crochet Straw Basket Bag

    Oscar de la Renta

    If you're planning on making a major poolside statement, this textured tote by Oscar de la Renta is a retro floral extravaganza. The rustic fringing feels earthy, and will mask your essentials if you prefer them out of sight. Style with a black strapless belted swimsuit and a printed kimono.

  • Falabella Tote Bag

    Stella McCartney

    This cool-girl option by Stella McCartney fuses the classic crafting technique with a touch of luxe hardware. Created from ivory cotton, it feels fresh and summery, and the soft vegetarian suede lining is a beautiful, functional touch.

  • Bon Bon Beaded Bucket bag

    Jimmy Choo

    Living up to its name, Jimmy Choo's Bon Bon bag is immeasurably sweet. Team with minimalist flats and a floaty maxi to allow your accessory to take centre stage. 

  • Crochet Shoulder Bag

    This chic blue shoulder bag by Mango would make for an excellent addition to any festival packing list. Team with tinted shades and low-rise trousers for the ultimate Y2K moment.

  • Pure Cotton Crochet Shopper

    Inject your weekly shop with a splash of colour with this pink and orange piece by M&S. The colours are utterly joyful and the design is super roomy.
