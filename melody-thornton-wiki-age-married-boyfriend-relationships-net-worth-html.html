Career of Melody:

Melody has established her name in the field of music; she is a singer, songwriter, and dancer too. She first received recognition when she sang a cover of Mariah Carey’s song in a talent show held at Papago Elementary School.

However, the major turning point came in her career after she became the member of the much-acclaimed group, ‘Pussycat Dolls’ in 2003. Melody heard about the audition after she completed her high school and came out victorious from the test.

She was the second lead singer of the girl group and being the youngest of all; the band members used to call her, ‘Baby Doll.’ Her group, under the leadership of Nicole Scherzinger, received immense fame from the songs like "Don't Cha," "Buttons," and "Stickwitu."

‘Pussycat Dolls’ earned Grammy Awards under various categories over the years; it won Best Pop Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocals in 2011 and best Contemporary Group Performance in 1968.

In 2010, she left the band with the intentions of focusing on her solo career. On March 15, 2012, she released her first mixtape, P.O.Y.B.L.

She has also faced the camera and appeared on many shows including ‘Keeping Up with the Kardashians,’ ‘Bank of Hollywood,’ ‘Celebs Go Dating,’ and ‘Celebrity Island with Bear Grylls.’
