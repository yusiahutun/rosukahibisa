Frequently Asked Questions

What Does Texture Spray Actually Do?

"Texturizing sprays add volume, lift, and definition to the hair," explains Hong. "It gives grip to the hair to hold movement and texture giving it a nice beachy and tousled look."

The stylist adds that all hair types and textures can benefit from a texture spray: "From fine to thick hair, texture sprays will add lift and movement." They can also simulate both dry shampoo and hairspray, depending on the formula. "It also absorbs some oils which makes the hair matted down. Texture sprays will boost any type of styling and is also great for creating updos and messy buns," she says.

12

How Do You Use Texture Spray?

Hearst Owned

"Spray it about six inches away starting at the roots," says Hong. "Then, throughout the hair, shake and tousle into place." When in doubt, says the stylist, "Everyone can benefit from a little texture spray."
