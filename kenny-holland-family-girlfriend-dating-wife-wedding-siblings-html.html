Kenny Holland Adds Girlfriend To Family; From Dating To Wedding!

Publish date: 2024-09-25
EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: February 18, 2020

Kenny Holland is a recording artist, singer, and songwriter best known for his singles, So What (2012), One Day at a Time (2012), and Backseat (2012). He began singing from a very young age. By the age of 19, he began touring the country. 

He also has an eponymous YouTube channel with over 475K subscribers. There, he often posts covers and his original works. 

Kenny Holland’s Age & Family

Kenny was born to Ken McCormick and Kelly McCormick on 1st May 1995 in Vernon, Arizona. His father is a loan specialist working with American Financial Lending, Inc. 

The young musician also has five siblings, all sisters.

Kenny Holland with his sisters in 2016 (Photo: Kenny Holland's Instagram)

They are named Madison, Camry, London, Bria, and Capri. 

Wife

The pop singer is married to Nicole Mitchell. They got engaged back in July of 2017 and tied the knot on 29th September 2017. 

Kenny Holland with wife Nicole McCormick on Haloween of 2017 (Photo: Kenny Holland's Twitter)

Just a day before the wedding, Kenny shared the proposal moment in a video titled “PROPOSING TO MY DREAM GIRL 800 flowers” with his fans via YouTube. 

Kenny Holland’s Bio

The YouTuber was born and raised in a musician family. He started learning to play the piano when he was eight. When he reached the age of 14, he began performing professionally.
