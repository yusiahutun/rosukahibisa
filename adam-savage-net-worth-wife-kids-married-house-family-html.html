American actor and model designer, Adam Savage is recognized for his performance in Myth Busters. Further, the actor has also designed models for several movies like The Matrix Reloaded and Star Wars: Attack of the Clones. 

However, when he's not busy in his movie projects, Adam Savage is a lovely husband and a father too. The married man, Adam is living happily with his long-time wife along with two children.  

Met Wife Online; Looks Ex-Partner's Children With Wife

Adam is a married man, who tied the knot with Julia Ward in 2002. Before he got married to wife Julia, Adam was committed to a relationship. It was only then, he first met Julia online and immediately developed a liking for each other. 

He has twin kids, Addison and Riley from his former affair and shares joint custody with his ex-partner. While Adam's kids visit their father, the children are looked after by Adam and his wife, Julia. 

The 50-year-old actor travels a lot for his work, but his busy schedule hasn't stopped him from spending quality times with his family. His love for his wife also hasn't diminished despite remaining married for nearly 16 years. The pair is also spotted regularly on public events.

While attending the red carpet event of the 2016's Creative Arts Emmys, Adam was joined by his wife, Julia. 

Adam and wife Julia attend the red carpet at the 2016 Creative Arts Emmys (Photo: Emmys.com)

Most recently on 4 October 2017, the couple attended the premiere of the movie Blade Runner 2049 and were even pictured on the event. Adam even took his movie moment to Instagram on 4 October 2017.

Filmmaker Father To Actress Sister; Net Worth In Millions

He started his acting career appearing in his father's show Sesame Street. Adam's sister, Kate Savage is also an actress. Adam hasn't mentioned much about his mother. Many assume that he wants his mother out of the spotlight to safeguard her personal space. Adam has created a fairy-land for kids in his home
