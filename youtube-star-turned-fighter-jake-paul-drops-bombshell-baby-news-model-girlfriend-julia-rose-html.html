YouTube star turned fighter Jake Paul drops bombshell baby news with model girlfriend Julia Rose

Publish date: 2024-09-22

YouTuber-turned-boxer Jake Paul has announced he is planning to start a family with model girlfriend Julia Rose despite the couple having a very public - and very recent - falling out.

The famous pair are known for being extremely close and post many images of their PDAs on their respective Instagram accounts.

However, in March they unfollowed each other on social media and were seen out with different people. Despite this, Paul denied they ever broke up, saying 'We were never "not together".'

Jake Paul and Julia Rose have announced that they are going to have a baby and start a family

Now, the on-again, off-again couple are ready to take their relationship to the next level.


Paul said on his new show BS With Jake Paul that the couple are talking about having a baby and starting a family.

'Julia and I are actually having conversations on like, when to get pregnant,' he revealed.
