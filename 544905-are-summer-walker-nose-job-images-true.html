Who is Summer Walker?

Summer Walker, of Liberian ancestry, was born in Atlanta, Georgia, on April 11, 1986. From 2016 to 2018, she managed a small cleaning company while simultaneously exploring the world of exotic dance. Her passion for music inspired her to learn guitar skills via YouTube tutorials, laying the foundation for her musical career.

In late 2017, she signed a deal with Atlanta-based label Love Renaissance. In 2018, she released her debut commercial mixtape, Last Day of Summer, featuring the breakout single Girls Need Love, which surged into the Billboard Hot 100. Notably, she collaborated with Canadian rapper Drake to remix the track.

With platinum-rated albums like Over It (2019) and Still Over It (2021-present), Summer Walker continues to captivate audiences with her soulful sound and talent.
