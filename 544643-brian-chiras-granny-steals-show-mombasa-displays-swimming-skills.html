Brian Chira's Granny Steals Show in Mombasa as She Displays Swimming Skills

Publish date: 2024-04-16
  • Brian Chira's grandmother, Esther Njeri, showed off her swimming skills as she enjoyed her vacation
  • She travelled to the coast with Chira's cousins and Baba Talisha in a getaway sponsored by Simon Kabu
  • As she spent quality time with her family in the swimming pool, Njeri looked happy and content in the nice pictures

The grandmother to the late TikToker Brian Chira is having the time of her life in Mombasa county.

Esther Njeri headed to the coast to enjoy a vacation sponsored by Bonfire Adventures CEO Simon Kabu.

Kabu offered a fully paid vacation to Chira's grandmother, Chira's cousin, and Baba Talisha, who has been instrumental in Chira's granny's life since his passing.

Brian Chira's grandma enjoys vacation

"Baba Talisha, Brian Chira's grandmother and cousins enjoying vacation," read the caption of a post online.

In one picture shared, Njeri was enjoying a swim with Chira's cousin and looked comfortable and composed.

While the little ones used floaters, she was floating on her own as she tailed slightly behind the kids.

She also looked happy as she enjoyed herself in the swimming pool, spending quality time with her family.

Chira's grandmother's tribute to late TikToker

Separately, family, friends, and followers of Chira bid farewell to the beloved TikTok personality in a touching tribute.

Chira, known for his vibrant presence on TikTok, was laid to rest amidst tears and heartfelt reminiscences.
