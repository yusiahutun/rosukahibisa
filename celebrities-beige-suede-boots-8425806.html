Martha Stewart and Jennifer Lopez Both Wear This Ageless Shoe Style

Publish date: 2024-09-28

What do Kate Hudson, Jennifer Lopez, and Martha Stewart all have in common? Yes, they’re all household names who are incredibly talented, incredibly beautiful, and incredibly, well, #goals in every way, but aside from these obvious similarities, there’s one thing I’ve noticed that the powerhouses share — and it pertains to their style. Drum roll please…

Before I get to the same-same thing, though, I understand if you’re confused, because honestly, no, I wouldn’t say Stewart and Lopez share a similar taste in clothing. The former goes cozy with classic sweaters and easygoing bottoms while the latter is known to push the limit with her clothing. Hudson also has a taste that’s all her own (just look at those controversial shoes she just wore). But alas, some pieces are just too good that everyone, regardless of their style preferences, simply falls in love — and in this case, I’m talking about beige suede boots that are a truly ageless footwear trend you need to try ASAP. 

Cole Haan Hampshire Waterproof Bootie

Dolce Vita Ulyses Platform Bootie

Stewart wore her beige suede boots back in September, and in the coolest way, too. She paired the versatile booties with a sheer maxi dress that color coordinated with her footwear to a T. Hudson recently wore super practical beige boots that had a chunky block heel and a front zipper for comfortable, easy wearing all day long, while Lopez paired her sky-high beige shoes with ultra-baggy jeans, because yes, the bigger the jeans, the better.

While all these sightings prove that the beige suede boot is certainly a force to be reckoned with, they also exhibit the inherent versatility and agelessness of the style. Stewart wore them! Hudson wore them! Lopez wore them! And mark my words: So many more celebs are going to hop aboard this shoe train because even though complicated, “ugly” footwear is still very much buzzy and happening, sometimes, the simpler, the better — and beige suede boots are perhaps as easy and unfussy as they get.

Black boots are an obvious must in your rotation, but TBH, I’d say beige boots fall into this same category of timeless essentials. Much like black boots, beige boots look great with jeans, skirts, trousers, dresses, shorts — there are infinite and endless ways to wear them, just take the aforementioned celebs as proof.
