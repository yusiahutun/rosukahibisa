Burning Desire Walkthrough

When you head to the side job marker for this mission, you’ll come across someone hiding behind a bush. This character is a cameo played by YouTuber, Jesse Cox. You can find more Cyberpunk 2077 references like this on IGN's Easter Eggs and References page. Regardless, the character named Jesse will beg for help while holding their crotch. It turns out they have a defective implant that’s causing them pain and they need to see a Ripperdoc.

So long as you pick to help them, the mission will continue on. If you choose not to, the mission will be permanently failed. You’ll need to drive Jesse to a Ripperdoc clinic where they can get some help. In spite of the objective saying to wait for the distressed man to call you, it will be a while before they do. It’s possible passing time in the main menu may trigger the next mission objective. Otherwise, work on other missions or activities in the meantime.

Once you get a call later from the Flaming Crotch Man, they’ll let you know that they transferred the money to you. There are a few conversation choices to ask how he’s doing. And he’ll also mention planning to get another implant in place of the previous one. After the call ends, the Burning Desire side job will be complete
