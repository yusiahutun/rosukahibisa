Nicki Minaj And Post Malone To Headline Jay-Zs Made In America Festival

Publish date: 2024-02-02

PHILADELPHIA, PA (CelebrityAccess) — The Jay-Z curated Made in America festival will return to Philadelphia for its seventh year on Labor Day Weekend.

The two-day fest, produced by Roc Nation, will feature headliners Nicki Minaj and Post Malone and is set to take place at Benjamin Franklin Parkway on Sept. 1st and 2nd.

In addition to musical entertainment, the festival also serves as a platform to positively impact the communities involved. Since the inaugural two-day concert in 2012, the event has generated $31.9 million in economic impact for the city.

This year, the festival will continue to benefit the ACLU of Pennsylvania as well as United Way of Greater Philadelphia and Southern New Jersey.
