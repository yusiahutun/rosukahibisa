Candice Bergen's Changing Looks

1952

Pigtails for the 4-year-old "Candy" Bergen.

1965

The fresh-faced 19-year-old dropped out of the University of Pennsylvania to act and model.

1967

She made the most of her perfect profile as a mod tourist in The Day the Fish Came Out.

1968

For her role as the Greek goddess Artemis in The Magus, she wore dramatic eye-accenting liner and frosted lipstick.

1977

Her clean and classic look was on display in The Domino Principle.

1978

The Oliver's Story star was snapped at the premiere sporting barely-there makeup and shiny waves.

1985

She presented the Academy Award for Best Visual Effects in a golden gown and gilded lips. Later that year she gave birth to daughter Chloe Malle.

1989

The Murphy Brown star twisted her pumped-up mane into an '80s updo.

1996

She picked up a People's Choice Award sporting high-volume ash-blond hair.

1998

At the Emmys, she showed off a short crop. "It's heavier on top, layered around the edges so that there are no severe lines-that face of hers is so beautiful, she can do anything," her hairstylist Eric Barnard told InStyle.

2002

A longer, layered bob for the Sweet Home Alabama premiere. "I like the bangs to move-that's what's sexy," hairstylist Jonathan Hanousek said of the cut.

2006

Nominated for her role in Boston Legal, Bergen attended the Emmys with a slight flip to her shoulder-length strands.

2011

For the N.Y.C. premiere of Miral, Bergen highlighted her cheekbones with peach blush.
