Who is Joe Greene:

Joe Greene is a famous Football Player. He was born on September 24, 1946 and his birthplace is Elgin, TX. Joe is also well known as, Dominant NFL defensive tackle who was known as Mean Joe Greene on the gridiron. He won four Super Bowl Championships with the Pittsburgh Steelers, with whom he spent his entire 13-year career.

Joe is originated from United States. He joined LC Greenwood, Ernie Holmes, and Dwight White on the dominant Steelers defensive line that became known as the Steel Curtain.
