Early Life of Bill Bellamy

Bill Bellamy stepped on this earth in 1965, on April 7, in New Wark, New Jersey, the United States of America. He is the son of Edna and William Bellamy Sr. As of now he is 57 years old. Similarly, he follows the Christianity religion.

Bellamy’s Siblings

He has two siblings named Karen Bellamy and Julius Jermaine Bellamy. His brother Julius died on 2014, November 22, and his mother also passed away in 2017. As a child, Bill grew up in a very supportive family. Bill holds an American nationality and belongs to the black ethnic group.

He initially attended Seton Hall Preparatory School in West Orange (formerly called South Orange), New Jersey. After that, he enrolled at Rutgers University to study economics.
