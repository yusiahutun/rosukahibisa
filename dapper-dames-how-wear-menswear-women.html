Dapper Dames: How to Wear Menswear for Women

Publish date: 2024-04-06

Dapper Dames

When it comes to suiting up, it seems unfair for the boys to have all the fun. When girls don menswear, and add a touch of glam, it’s an instant style boost—and man, do they make it sexy. Every once in a while even the girliest girl should take a page from the male style bible by flirting with the masculine look. Because, c’mon look at Cara Delevingne in this Topshop suit: don’t you want to show the guys how it’s really done?

Topshop: Tailored suit blazer, $808; cigarette trousers, $275.
Asia Standard Tower, 59 Queen’s Rd. Central, 2118-5353.
 

Diane Keaton, Tilda Swinton, Anne Hathaway in that Lanvin tux at the Oscars: glamorous, chic, effortless. Getting the androgynous look right can be tricky, so start small by pairing outfits with oxfords or skater shoes, and before you know it you’ll be suiting up for your next dinner date.

HOT OPENING

Toms Boys

Everyone’s favorite socially conscious brand Toms is opening up a flagship store at IFC this week and it looks hipsterific—you know, unfinished wood panelling and Aztec patterned rugs. I thought Toms were simply summer shoes (which smell by September), but the new fall line proves me wrong. The dark hues and strong patterns are perfect as a weekend shoe—and the sunglasses are pretty great too.

Shop 3092B, 3/F, IFC Mall, 8 Finance St., Central, 2682-8699, www.facebook.com/TOMSHongKong.
