Jaclyn Smith, 69, Stuns in Kmart Campaign with Daughter Spencer Margaret

Publish date: 2024-04-10

Like mother, like daughter!

Jaclyn Smith and her lookalike daughter, Spencer Margaret Richmond, are the faces of the 30th anniversary campaign of the veteran actress' Kmart collection -- and we're taking you behind the scenes at their photo shoot.

In one pic, the 69-year-old star looks fun and flirty in a leopard-print pencil skirt with a black leather jacket as her 29-year-old daughter rocks the same print in a chic shift dress.

Another stylish snapshot shows the fashionable duo wearing matching cream trench coats. These two look like sisters!

The "Charlie's Angels" star, who famously played Kelly Garrett on the popular '70s show, launched the affordable line back in 1985.

Smith recently revealed the secret to her collection's longevity -- creating timeless pieces. "The pieces need to go from day to night," she told PEOPLE. "I'm a mom, I'm traveling, I need to feel comfortable all day. The fabrics need to look good, but really, they need to feel good or I won't wear it."

When Jaclyn was asked about how she has aged so gracefully, her answer had nothing to do with her beauty routine.

"Being loved," she declared. "I had incredible parents, I have an amazing husband, two wonderful children — that's really the key to it, being loved."
