French Montana Denies Cheating on His Ex with Khloe Kardashian Amid Jordyn-Tristan Scandal

Publish date: 2024-05-12

The rapper speaks out after he's dragged into the latest Kardashian drama.

French Montana claims he was "single like a dollar bill" when he starting dating Khloe Kardashian, after his name got dragged into the current cheating scandal between Khloe and Tristan Thompson.

Although the rapper and reality star dated years ago and broke up in 2014, French name came up after Jordyn Woods' "Red Table Talk" episode dropped on Friday. Jordyn, who used to be best friends with Kylie Jenner, has been accused of hooking up with Khloe's baby daddy while they were still together. Woods claimed he kissed her, but nothing else happened.

On Thursday, the night before Jordyn's interview dropped, Perez Hilton noted the similarities between Khloe's current situation and her past relationship with French Montana. He tweeted, "Did Khloe Kardashian start dating French Montana when he was still with Trina? And wasn't Khloe friends with Trina???"

The subject came up again on Friday, after the interview was released on Facebook Watch. During the talk, the 21-year-old model claimed she wasn't the reason Khloe and Tristan split, something which set off Khloe on social media.

Trina also alluded to the situation on her Instagram, posting a photo of herself with the caption, "Minding the business that pays me."
