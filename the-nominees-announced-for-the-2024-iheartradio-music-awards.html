The Nominees Announced For The 2024 iHeartRadio Music Awards

Publish date: 2024-06-06

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — iHeartMedia and their broadcast partners FOX Entertainment announced the nominees for the 2024 iHeartRadio Music Awards with Taylor Swift, Doja Cat, Morgan Wallen, and Dua Lipa all in the running for top honors.

The annual awards gala will honor artists and songs who have had the heaviest rotation this year on iHeartRadio stations across the U.S., and on their streaming app throughout 2023.

The show will feature award presentations, live performances from some of the top artists in modern music as well as honoring artists for standout achievements over the course of their career.

Artists receiving multiple nominations this year include, 21 Savage, Beyoncé, Billie Eilish, Blink-182, Carin León, David Kushner, Doechii, Doja Cat, Drake, Dua Lipa, Fall Out Boy, Foo Fighters, Grupo Frontera, Ice Spice, Jelly Roll, Jung Kook, Karol G, Lil Durk, Luke Combs, Megan Moroney, Metro Boomin, Miley Cyrus, Morgan Wallen, Noah Kahan, Olivia Rodrigo, Paramore, Peso Pluma, Rema, Selena Gomez, Shakira, SZA, Taylor Swift, The Weeknd, Tyla, Usher and Yng Lvcas.

“Our iHeartRadio Music Awards are special because they’re truly reflective of what listeners love,” said Tom Poleman, Chief Programming Officer for iHeartMedia. “We’re excited to share the journey on how they became the biggest songs and celebrate these amazing artists with music fans across the nation together LIVE from Los Angeles.”

The awards gala will take place at the DOlby Theatre in Los Angeles on April 1st and will be aired live (with a tape delay in some markets) on Fox. The event also will be heard on iHeartRadio stations nationwide and on the iHeartRadio app.

“The iHeartRadio Music Awards is a night where we celebrate the biggest artists and songs fans loved listening to on the radio all year long,” said John Sykes, President of Entertainment Enterprises for iHeartMedia. “We’ll also share stories from the artists themselves about their hard-fought journeys to the top of the charts.”
