Early life

The TV host was born on 12 April 1971 (age 51 as of 2022) in Memphis, Tennessee, United States. She is the daughter of Rosa Elizabeth Doherty (mother) and John Thomas Doherty Jr. (father). Her mother worked as a beauty parlour owner, and her father was a mortgage consultant.

Shannen comes from Irish ancestry on her father’s side and Scottish, English, and Scots-Irish ancestries from her mother’s side. She also has a brother named Sean Doherty.

While growing up, Shannen Doherty’s parents raised her as a Christian of the Southern Baptist faith. She attended Heritage Christian High School, where her passion for acting started. When she turned ten, she became a child actress and successfully converted her role to a full-time actress in adulthood.

Professional career

Shannen started acting when she turned ten. First, she landed guest spots on TV shows, including Voyagers! and Father Murphy. Then, the actress made a name for herself when she was 11 after playing Jenny Wilder in Little House on the Prairie.

After her performance as Jenny Wilder, she appeared in many more shows and films, including Our House (1986) and Heathers (1988). Doherty’s success as an actress started in 1990 when she played Brenda Walsh in Beverly Hills: 90210. She became a household name and subsequently appeared in the thriller Almost Dead (1994) and Mallrats (1995).

Shannen continued her success as a film and TV actress throughout the 2000s and 2010s. In 2021, she portrayed Diana Carroll in List of a Lifetime. She still has several upcoming projects for 2022, including How to Make a Deal with the Devil.

Shannen Doherty's husband and children

Shannen has been in many relationships. In early 1993, she was the fiancé of Dean Jay Factor, the heir of Max Factor. Unfortunately, Dean filed for a restraining order on 25 May 1993, alleging physical violence and threats on the part of Doherty. The restraining order automatically ended their relationship. She also dated Judd Nelson the same year, but the romance was short.

On 11 October 1993, Shannen married Ashley Hamilton, an American actor and director known for Iron Man 3 (2013). But that marriage never lasted long. Instead, the two divorced several months later, in April 1994. After the divorce, she dated Rob Weiss for four years, between 1994 and 1998. Unfortunately, the two did not get married.

Shannen waited for another three years and dated Julian McMahon. And like her previous relationships, her romance with Julian ended the same year. In 2002, Shannen married Rick Salomon. Unfortunately, the marriage ended nine months after the couple decided to annul it. Then, between 2004 and 2005, she was in a relationship with Jason Pomeranc but the two never married.

What is Shannen Doherty's health update?

Shannen has battled many illnesses throughout her lifetime. In 1999, doctors diagnosed her with Crohn’s disease. Then, in 2015, doctors told her she had breast cancer that had spread to her lymph nodes. A year later, she received several treatments, including a mastectomy. Unfortunately, the surgery showed that some cancer cells had spread beyond her lymph nodes.

The cancer was now more advanced, forcing her to undergo chemotherapy and radiotherapy. Luckily, on 29 April 2017, Shannen announced that the cancer was in remission. However, three years later, on 4 February 2020, she gave the bad news that her cancer had returned in 2019 and had reached stage four.
