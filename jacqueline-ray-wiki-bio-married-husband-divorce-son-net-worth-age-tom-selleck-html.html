Moreover, in 2016, Jacqueline' ex-husband, Tom opened up to RadarOnline about how his memories of married life with her came back to life during the screening of 'made for cable' series. He emotionally opened up saying that their 'bust-up' was the worst thing ever, 

“The bust-up was the worst thing that ever happened to me. I thought I’d be married for always.”

“Tom is a very charming guy. He fell in love with the island and with the island girls.”

“I’ve found it very difficult to expose my private life and live with it. It’s important to nurture your marriage. I think wanting time away from work has been good for that.”

On the other hand, Jacqueline was devastated by Tom's action,

[She] “was crushed by the way their marriage hit the skids when he hit the big time — and thought it showed how fickle Tom could be. She never suspected Tom would dump her after she had stood behind him during all the lean years when he was a struggling actor looking for a major role.”

However, Jacqueline tied the knot for the third time with Clarence Barry Witmer on March 21 in the year 1992 with whom she shares a daughter, Umeko. But again, the information on her third married life, as well as the identity of her third husband, remains private within the couple.

As for her ex-husband Tom, he's married to his present wife Jillie Jean Mack for about thirty years now. Tom and Jillie share a daughter together.

Meanwhile, in early 2014, Jacqueline got herself into a pile of controversy after the police arrested her for hiring a stranger to murder her son-in-law. She claimed that she was afraid for her daughter, who was suffering abuse and violence from her husband, Leon Baucham.
