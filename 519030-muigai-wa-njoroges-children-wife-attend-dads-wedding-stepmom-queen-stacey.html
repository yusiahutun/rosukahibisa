Muigai wa Njoroge's Children with First Wife Attend Dad's Wedding with Stepmom Queen Stacey

Publish date: 2024-05-25
  • It was pomp and colour at Ndeiya village, Kiambu county, as Muigai wa Njoroge and his second wife, Queen Stacey, tied the knot
  • The lovebirds' traditional wedding was attended by several politicians and celebrities and emceed by philanthropist Karangu Muraya
  • Muigai's children, both from first and second families, graced the event and showed their two parents with love, wishing them well

Gospel singer Muigai wa Njoroge and his second wife, Queen Stacey, have tied the knot in a colourful traditional wedding.

Did Muigai's first wife Njeri attend his wedding with Stacey?

Muigai and Stacey's wedding was convened on Saturday, August 26, and was attended by several politicians and celebrities among them Limuru MP John Kiragu, MCA Kung'u Smart and Henry Desagu. Philanthropist Muraya Karangu emceed the glamourous ceremony.

The singer's eight children, both from his first marriage with Njeri and second wife with Stacey, pampered them with love, describing their dad as an amazing person.

One by one, they stood and pampered their parents with love. Muigai and Stacey's first son opened the floor, and his British accent left guests awed.

"I love you, mom. My father is an amazing man. Everything he has done, he has done it for us.I thank you from the bottom of my heart. May God bless you in your life. I love you dad and mom," he said before hugging his parents.

Two of Njeri's daughters followed and wished his father and stepmom with love.

Stacey's second child, a daughter, was next but only greeted guests before returning to sit down. Then Muigai and Njeri's second-born son, Prince Kinyanjui, thanked guests who graced his parents' wedding.

"...I still love you (Stacey) like my mom. I respect you so much. As parents, you two have never let us down. Congratulations on this big day. I will do everything right because I know I will marry soon," he said leaving guests in stiches.

The couple's young children also said a word at the ceremony.

Muigai's first wife, Njeri, was nowhere to be seen.

Muigai's flashy birthday

Recently, Stacey threw her man a lavish all-wite birthday party and shared lovely photos of them together at the event.

Top Kikuyu celebs, among them musicians and businesspeople, attended. The couple's youngest daughter was present, too.

Muigai was gifted loads of cash in KSh 1,000 denominations by his wife. Muigai's beautiful second wife shared photos on her social handles, and her fans noted that Stacey looked beautiful.

The singer's first wife, Njeri, was missing in action, and fans questioned her whereabouts.
