5 Reasons Classic Watches Never Go Out of Style

Publish date: 2024-06-23

Most modern watches are powered by electricity, but many collectible "status" watches still run on old school mechanics. What makes them tick? Brilliant craftsmanship, spinning cogs, gears, and countless tiny springs, all assembled by hand. Now that's a smart watch.

TIP: Even the daintiest wrist can rock a chronograph-inspired watch that adds a masculine glamour to any outfit. Look for an oversize face, chain link strap, and extra dials.
