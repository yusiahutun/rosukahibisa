Who is Tig Notaros wife Stephanie Allynne?

Publish date: 2024-04-17

Stephanie Allynne is the wife of Tig Notaro, an American stand-up comedian, writer, radio contributor, and actress.

Tig Notaro met her wife, Stephanie Allynne, on the set of the film In a World. They got engaged on January 1, 2015 and married on October 24, 2015. They had twin sons in 2016, conceived through a surrogate using Allynne’s eggs.

Stephanie Allynne is best known for her roles in the films In a World… (2013), Dream Corp, LLC (2016), One Mississippi (2016-17), and The L Word: Generation Q (2019–present).

Stephanie Allynne is a regular performer with ASSSSCAT, Wild Horses, and the Last Day of School: The Text Message show.

Stephanie Allynne began appearing on television shows such as The League, The Mindy Project, Happy Endings, 2 Broke Girls, The Kroll Show, and Key & Peele.

With her wife Tig Notaro, Allynne wrote and co-starred in One Mississippi. The show was essentially an autobiography of their lives.

Allynne is working on an hour-long drama for HBO, as well as co-writing and producing the Netflix film First Ladies, starring Jennifer Aniston and Tig Notaro.
