If you’ve been anticipating the comeback of some of New York City’s greatest Housewives, you may be wondering how to watch RHONY Legacy online to see Ramona, LuAnn, Dorinda, and more New York veterans return after the original series was rebooted.

RHONY Legacy is the fourth season of The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip, a spin-off of Bravo’s Real Housewives franchise which started in 2006 with The Real Housewives of Orange County. Since then, Bravo has aired 10 more franchises: The Real Housewives of New York City, The Real Housewives of Atlanta, The Real Housewives of New Jersey, Real Housewives of D.C., The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, The Real Housewives of Miami, The Real Housewives of Potomac, The Real Housewives of Dallas, The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City, and The Real Housewives of Dubai.

While The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip started as a crossover featuring Housewives from different Real Housewives franchises, RHONY Legacy is the first Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip season to feature former housewives from one location: New York City. The Real Housewives of New York City premiered in 2008 and aired for 13 seasons until it was rebooted in 2023 with a whole new cast featuring Jenna Lyons, Erin Lichy, Sai De Silva, Ubah Hassan, Brynn Whitfield and Jessel Taank.

So where can Bravoholics stream The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip: RHONY Legacy? Read on for how to watch RHONY Legacy to see the return of all of your favorite (and least favorite) New York housewives.

How to Watch RHONY Legacy Online

How can Bravo fans watch RHONY Legacy online? The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip: RHONY Legacy is available to stream on Peacock, which offers two plans: Peacock Premium for $5.99 per month and Peacock Premium Plus for $11.99 per month. Peacock also offers a yearly plan of Peacock Premium for $59.99 per year (which saves users around $12 from the monthly plan) and Peacock Premium Plus for $119.99 per year (which saves users around $24 from the monthly plan.)

Peacock Premium, the most popular plan, offers more than 80,000 hours of TV, movies, and sports, including Peacock originals like The Real Housewives Ultimatum Girls Trip; live sports and events; current episodes of NBC and Bravo shows; and 50 live channels. Peacock Premium Plus offers everything included in Peacock Premium, along with no ads; the ability to download and watch select titles offline; and live, 24/7 access to your local NBC channel. Read on for step-by-step instructions on how to watch RHONY Legacy.

  • Visit PeacockTV.com
  • Click “Pick a Plan”
  • Select your plan between Peacock Premium and Peacock Premium Plus
  • Enter your information and payment method
  • Log into your account
  • Search for The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip and start watching

    • Who’s in the RHONY Legacy cast?

    The RHONY Legacy cast includes six former Housewives from the original Real Housewives of New York City, which ran for 13 seasons from 2008 to 2021 before it was rebooted in Season 14 with new Housewives: Sai De Silva, Ubah Hassan, Erin Lichy, Jenna Lyons, Jessel Taank, and Brynn Whitfield. See the RHONY Legacy cast below.

    • Ramona Singer (Season 1 – 13)
    • Luann de Lesseps (Season 1 – 13)
    • Kelly Killoren Bensimon (Season 2 – 4)
    • Sonja Morgan (Season 3 – 13)
    • Dorinda Medley (Season 7 – 13)
    • Kristen Taekman (Season 6 – 7)

    In an interview with Daily Beast in December 2023, Luann explained why she was “apprehensive” to say yes to starring in RHONY Legacy. “When it was for Legacy, I was probably more apprehensive about that, because there’s so much more to share. You get into what storylines [you had] and life, as opposed to just going on a girls’ trip for a week. I was a lot happier—let’s put it that way—to actually do [this show] instead,” she said.

    She also told the site about whether there will be more RHONY Legacy seasons after The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip Season 4. “I know the fans want to have more series with us. Maybe [it can just be] OGs are just traveling—going on Below Deck, finally getting those Latin lovers in Argentina. I just feel like it was time,” she said. “We’ve had a great run. I moved onto Crappie Lake, I’m very busy with my cabaret career, which is kicking off a new tour in February, by the way. I have children! I’ve been super busy, so a week on vacation with the OGs was just perfection for me.”

    Bensimon also addressed her drama with Taekman during RHONY Legacy. “I can’t speak for her. I can speak to myself. All of the fans know I’m 100-percent authentic. I come into the room and I am who I am and I bring every single thing that came through that door with me—good, bad, or ugly. If I’m in a bad mood, I’m in a bad mood,” she said. “I don’t just turn on for the cameras, because I don’t do that, period. I’ve always been myself and I think that’s one thing the fans really like about me: They can rely on me. I’m not going to Google search [myself], I’m not going to watch every show back and forth [like Kristen says she does in RHONY Legacy], and I’m not going to figure out what people’s storylines are. I know if you’re a good person, believe me. I’ve got a very good radar for humans.”
