Lady Gaga Just Wore the Tallest Platform Boots

Publish date: 2024-06-25
  • Lady Gaga stepped out in New York this week wearing an epic pair of platform boots.
  • She completed her look with an Alberta Ferretti jumpsuit and a Celine doctor's bag.
  • The Oscar-winner was in the city for Pride Live's Stonewall Day Concert, where she gave an impassioned speech to the LGBTQ+ community.

Lady Gaga regularly stuns us with her eclectic sense of style, and her latest trip to New York is no exception.

Related Story

Having stepped out in two iconic outfits during Pride, the singing superstar went for a more formal look as she left her hotel. Gaga wore a chic black jumpsuit by Alberta Ferretti, with a gold pleated shirt underneath. She carried her beloved Celine doctor's bag, but it was her platform boots that really caught our attention.

The A Star Is Born performer somehow managed to walk in the major platform heels, which was no minor feat:

James Devaney//Getty Images

Basically, Lady Gaga deserves a newfound respect for managing to make the hugest platform heels look like child's play. I legit would've face-planted the sidewalk wearing these, yet Gaga is graceful:

James Devaney//Getty Images

In her incredible Pride speech, Lady Gaga told the crowd, "I will continue to fight every day during shows, and even when I’m not on stage, to spread a message that’s actually quite simple—be kind." She continued, "So today—and I hope every day—dance, sing, rejoice, worship yourself, worship each other!”

She also said, "The universe brought us together in the spirit of kindness, and together we’re a powerhouse... I hope you celebrate every inch of who you are today. You were born this way and you are superstars!"
