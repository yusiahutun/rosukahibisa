Bruce has toygirl of his own

Publish date: 2024-06-21

When his ex-wife showed off her 25-year-old toyboy, Bruce Willis appeared less than impressed.

Now, however, he seems keen to follow Demi Moore's lead ... by dating a 25-year-old of his own.

The actor, 48, is seeing former Baywatch actress Brooke Burns.

The couple are believed to have met at a Rolling Stones concert last November, but only became an item last month.

"She didn't express any interest in Bruce, but he pursued her and eventually they started dating," said a friend last night.

"She doesn't mind the age gap as he is very young at heart.

"She thinks Bruce is very sexy and that's the most important thing.

"It is funny that Demi is also dating a 25-year-old, but it is not something they think about.

"They have met a few times at family occasions and she has admitted it felt a bit weird."

Willis, who has enjoyed a spot of snorkelling while on a family holiday on the Italian Riviera, reportedly told 40-year-old Miss Moore it was wrong to go out with someone young enough to be her son.

And, when pictured with his former wife - with whom he has three children - and her new love Ashton Kutcher, he could only manage a wry smile.

Miss Burns, who hosts the American version of television quiz Dog Eat Dog, is the latest beauty to be linked with the Die Hard star since the end of his 11-year marriage in 1998.

Willis has dated Spanish model Maria Bravo, 33, porn star Alisha Klass, 28, actress Estella Warren, 23, and former Miss Czech Republic Eva Jasanovska.

Miss Burns, meanwhile, has previously been out with Steve Bing, Elizabeth Hurley's ex, and Julian McMahon, the former husband of Dannii Minogue.

Miss Moore's romance with Kutcher has revived her acting career, while he has also gained from being in the limelight.

The actor has been offered a string of movie roles and was voted the world's most eligible bachelor by People magazine.
