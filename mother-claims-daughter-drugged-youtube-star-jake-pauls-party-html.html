Mother claims her daughter was 'drugged' at YouTube star Jake Paul's party

Publish date: 2024-06-03

A mother has claimed that her daughter and a group of teenage girls were drugged while attending a party at YouTube star Jake Paul's home on Saturday night. 

Paul, 22, threw a massive birthday party for the rapper Desiigner at his Calabasas, California mansion, documenting the entire night on his Instagram. 

Allegations of drugging first came to light on the private Facebook group 'Moms of Conejo Valley', which has more than 17,000 members, on Sunday. 

'A big warning to those with teenage daughters: My daughter was at a party in Calabsas [sic] last night for YouTuber Jake Paul,' the post reads.
