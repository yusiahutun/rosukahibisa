Inside 2022 CMT Music Awards host Kelsea Ballerini's jaw-dropping Nashville home

Publish date: 2024-07-20

The country music singer lives with her husband Morgan Evans

    Kelsea Ballerini will be giving fans a glimpse inside her stunning home after being forced to host Monday's CMT Music Awards from there following a positive test for COVID-19.

    MORE: Inside country music singers' jaw-dropping homes: Carrie Underwood, Jimmie Allen, Miranda Lambert + more

    The country music singer – who was due to join Marvel's Anthony Mackie on stage at Nashville's Municipal Auditorium, he will now co-host with nominee Kane Brown – lives in a beautiful modern townhouse in Nashville, Tennessee, that is filled with rustic touches. 

    WATCH: Kelsea Ballerini shows off her killer shoe collection inside her Nashville home

    The 2400-square foot home has four bedrooms and two-and-a-half bathrooms, and Kelsea reportedly purchased the property for $585,000.

    Being on the road for much of the year, Kelsea enlisted the help of interior design company, Laurel & Wolf, to decorate her new home, which has floor-to-ceiling windows, wooden floors, and soaring ceilings.

    MORE: Kelsea Ballerini stuns in sun-soaked bikini photo from impeccable Nashville home

    MORE: Kelsea Ballerini celebrates huge news as fans rush to congratulate her

    Each room follows a similar neutral palette of gray and white, with pops of color added to her wall art, quirky furnishings, and vivid floral wallpaper.

    Kelsea's home has a neutral color scheme 

    "When I was a kid, I loved bright colors. I think every room I had had hot pink walls," Kelsea told . "Now, I'm a lot more neutral."

    The property features sliding barn doors, a music room, a writing room, and an open plan dining, living, and kitchen area – which features black guitars hanging from the ceiling of the all-white space in a nod to her love of music.

    Kelsea lives with her husband, Morgan Evans

    Kelsea's master bedroom is decorated in soothing hues of navy, gray, and white, with splashes of royal blue accents like pillows and blankets on her bed.

    One room that is far from neutral though is Kelsea's walk-in closet and glam room which is covered in bright pink floral wallpaper. Her vanity space features a blush pink velvet bench, a black vintage desk with pink drawer handles, and alternative wall art.

    Kelsea's home boasts a spacious backyard

    Her open-plan closet boasts an elaborate chandelier and is meticulously organized, displaying all her clothes, including her favorite pair of hot pink heels she bought when she signed her record deal: "These were a celebration gift to myself, they were my good luck shoes," she added to the magazine.

    There also appears to be a light-filled conservatory

    But Kelsea's favorite room of the home is, unsurprisingly, her music room, which proudly displays her very first guitar hanging on the barn wood feature wall. "This is my favorite room in the world because I feel like it's such a heartbeat of everything I love," she said of the space.
