Doug Edley Joins UTA

Publish date: 2024-03-18

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — United Talent Agency has hired Douglas Edley to help bolster their touring division.

Edley, formerly an agent at Gersh, represents a roster of comic talent that includes Henry Cho, Eddie Griffin, and Jordan Peele of Key & Peele, though no announcement regarding the disposition of his artist roster post-move has been released.

According to The Wrap, Edley is one of a spate of agents recently picked up by UTA as the company appears to be looking to increase their cachet in the live touring world. Last week, agents Brian Swardstrom and Danie Streisand both joined UTA from William Morris Endeavor. – Staff Writers
