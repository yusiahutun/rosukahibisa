Long Island Medium's Theresa Caputo set to wow UK fans with messages from the dead

Publish date: 2024-04-30

TLC star Theresa Caputo will be coming over to the UK for the first time with Theresa Caputo Live: The Experience and has spoken to Daily Star about her show.

Caputo, star of TLC’s Long Island Medium, will be coming to the O2 from 23 - 24 June to share stories of her life and hopes to inspire others in the process.

She will explain how her incredible gift works and will be giving examples to the audience through healing messages.

For those interested in attending Caputo’s show, she will also walk throughout the audience and speak to many of those present.
