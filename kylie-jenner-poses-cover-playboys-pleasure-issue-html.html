'When we have our days off, we play with Stormi all day, and the three of us have a lot of fun together. We do a lot of swimming; Stormi loves swimming, so we’re always in the pool or playing in her room or taking her out to lunch with us,' said the daughter of Kris Jenner.

And she noted that even though they fight over what TV shows to watch 'we’re always laughing or getting into deep talks. '

The sister of Kendall Jenner began her interview by talking about the move to pose for the cover of the legendary magazine founded by Hugh Hefner. 'I never thought that I would pose for PLAYBOY, but I love the new covers.

'I love what playboy is doing right now creatively, and when you presented me with the idea—that you would be behind the cover and creative direction and be in control of the images—I thought it was a perfect fit, because I trust you and your vision,' she told Scott. She added that she goes to him for input because he has 'the craziest, coolest ideas.'

Jenner added that she has learned how to deal with the stress that comes with her fame: 'I do see myself as a really strong person and just to remain positive and try to find those core positive people in your life and stay close to your family and the people who want to see you do the best.' Kylie added having faith has helped her.

Also in the sprawling interview, she admits that she one day will expand her makeup and skincare company to maybe include wine and bed linens.

'We just launched Kylie Skin not too long ago, and it has been a huge success. I can’t wait to keep expanding and creating new things.'
